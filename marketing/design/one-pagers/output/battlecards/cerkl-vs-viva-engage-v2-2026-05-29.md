# Cerkl Broadcast vs. Viva Engage

Deliver consistent communications, prioritize what matters, and get deep insights across every channel.

<!-- component: battlecard (criterion rail | Viva Engage | Cerkl Broadcast · Omni AI) -->

**Column headers**
- **Viva Engage** — An employee community & engagement platform (Yammer successor)
- **Cerkl Broadcast · Omni AI** — An AI-powered communications platform built for the enterprise

**1. Communication Consistency** — *One message, delivered the right way, every time.*
- Viva (−): **Limited controls for consistency** — Built for community and conversation; one-to-many, governed messaging is harder to enforce for brand, message hierarchy and governance at scale.
- Cerkl (✓): **Enterprise-wide consistency by design** — Centralized content hub, templates, approval workflows and brand guardrails keep every message on-brand and aligned across channels and locations.

**2. Prioritizing What Matters** — *Cut through the noise; focus on what's important.*
- Viva (−): **Feeds are conversation-first** — Algorithmic feeds prioritize conversations and trending posts, making it difficult to ensure important announcements rise above the noise.
- Cerkl (✓): **AI-driven prioritization** — Omni AI surfaces the right content, at the right time, to the right people—so your most important messages get seen and acted on.

**3. In-Depth Insights & Analytics** — *Understand impact across every channel.*
- Viva (−): **Siloed, surface-level insights** — Community engagement metrics only—views, reactions, replies. No per-recipient read confirmation and no unified, cross-channel view of message impact.
- Cerkl (✓): **Comprehensive, cross-channel analytics** — Deep, unified insights across email, intranet, Teams, Slack and mobile—per-employee opens, reads and acknowledgments: what resonates, and who still needs it.

**4. Reach Across Every Channel** — *Meet employees where they are.*
- Viva (−): **Microsoft ecosystem confined** — Primarily within Microsoft 365 (desktop, mobile, Teams) and opt-in—employees who don't visit the feed miss the message. Limited reach to non-Microsoft channels.
- Cerkl (✓): **Truly omnichannel** — Publish once and reach everywhere—email, intranet/SharePoint, mobile app, Teams, Slack and microsites—your entire workforce, not just feed visitors.

**5. AI & Intelligence** — *Use AI to save time and improve results.*
- Viva (−): **Basic AI capabilities** — AI for content creation, summarization and sentiment within Engage, but no cross-channel personalization or delivery recommendations.
- Cerkl (✓): **Omni AI: your advantage, opt-in by design** — AI that works for you—content creation, personalization, audience intelligence and recommendations you switch on when ready. Assistive, never on autopilot.

**6. Total Value** — *Maximize impact and realize ROI.*
- Viva (−): **Add-on with limitations** — Requires Microsoft 365 licensing; additional tools often needed to achieve enterprise-grade communications and analytics.
- Cerkl (✓): **Stronger ROI, faster impact** — A purpose-built platform that drives higher engagement, better outcomes and clear ROI—without stitching together multiple tools.
