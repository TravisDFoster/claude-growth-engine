**Step 1 — Compliance.** We communicate because we have to, and the result reads like a legal notice. Most teams stay parked here.

**Step 2 — Clarity.** Time taken to explain the what, when, and why — written for the employee, not the approval chain.

**Step 3 — Relevance.** The same message in different shapes, segmented by life stage, role, and geography so it lands.

**Step 4 — Connection.** Benefits become tangible proof of the EVP — the receipt of the promise, not just the promise itself.
