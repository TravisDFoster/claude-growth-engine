## Body

**Insights is live in Broadcast.**

Starting June 20, every Broadcast account gets Insights, a ground-up rebuild of your analytics. Same data you trust. A completely new experience around it.

If you have ever opened a report, squinted at a chart, clicked three layers deep, lost your place, and given up before you found the number leadership asked for, this one is for you.

**What's new**

- **See it, not a spreadsheet's idea of it.** A wider range of visuals so trends and outliers are obvious at a glance, plus customizable tables for anyone who wants every column.
- **Navigation that gets out of your way.** Reworked pathways and breadcrumbs, so you can drill from a high-level summary down to a single subscriber's click history without losing the thread.
- **Faster load times.** Open a report, get your answer, stay in the question you were trying to answer.
- **Benchmark against yourself.** Every rate is compared to your own running average, so a 22% open rate stops being a number floating in space and starts meaning something.
- **Every channel in one place.** Channel-level reporting plus News Digest delivery preferences and category interests.
- **Dashboards you can share.** Build a clean view, hand it to a stakeholder, and control exactly who sees what.

**Why it matters**

You are measured on outcomes but rarely given time to be an analyst. Insights closes that gap. It gives you a clearer view of what's working, what isn't, and where to adjust, faster and with less friction, in a form you can hand to the people who need to see it.

There is nothing to install and nothing to wait for. The core email and content analytics are available on every plan, including Foundations.

The fastest way in: open Insights, look at one thing you already report on, your last all-staff Blast or your News Digest, and notice how much less work it takes to get the answer.
