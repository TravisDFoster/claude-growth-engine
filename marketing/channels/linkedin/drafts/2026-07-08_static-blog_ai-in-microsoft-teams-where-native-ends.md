## Caption

Copilot can draft your announcement, summarize the thread, and post it to the channel in seconds. It cannot tell you who read it.

For internal comms, that is the gap that matters. Teams confirms the send and stays silent on the read. A channel post proves the message exists somewhere people could see it, not that the 30 people who needed to act on a benefits deadline or a safety change are among the 8 who reacted with a thumbs-up. And the frontline crew who rarely open the app never had a chance to see it at all.

Native Teams is a strong collaboration tool. Comms delivery and measurement is a separate job: targeted delivery to the right audience, across more than one channel, with per-employee read tracking you can actually report on.

Our new post draws the line between where native Teams AI is enough and where you have outgrown it, plus the four signals that tell you which side you are on.
