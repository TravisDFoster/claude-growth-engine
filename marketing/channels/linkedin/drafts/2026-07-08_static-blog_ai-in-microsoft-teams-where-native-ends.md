## Caption

Copilot can draft your announcement, summarize the thread, and post it to the channel in seconds. It cannot tell you who read it.

For internal comms, that is the gap that matters. Teams confirms the send and stays silent on the read. A channel post proves a message exists somewhere people could see it. It does not prove the 30 people who needed to act on a benefits deadline are among the 8 who left a thumbs-up, and the frontline crew who rarely open the app never had a chance to see it.

Native Teams is a strong collaboration tool. Comms delivery and measurement is a separate job: targeted delivery to the right audience, across more than one channel, with per-employee read tracking you can report on.
