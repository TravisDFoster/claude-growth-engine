## Caption

Internal comms leaders, here's a question worth sitting with.

When leadership asks "did this land?", you probably reach for an open rate or a page-view number. That number reflects the channel you can measure. The audience you need to reach lives somewhere else.

If you publish across email, Teams, mobile, and intranet but only report from one of those channels, you have a structural blind spot. As of 2026, no public IC engagement benchmark even measures across channels.

So the honest answer is rarely the one in the dashboard.

What's your gut estimate?

## Poll
