## Caption

For financial services IC teams, every internal message is an engagement artifact and an audit artifact at the same time. The split between those two jobs sets the operational shape of the week.

Some weeks the heavy lift is regulatory compliance: feeding retention systems, evidencing receipt on attestations, chasing acknowledgment rates to 100. Some weeks it is audience segmentation: making sure information barriers held, that the AD groups still match the org chart, that no list quietly spans a restricted population. Some weeks it is the approvals queue: legal, compliance, and risk all reviewing the same send before it goes out.

All three are real work. Most weeks one of them is the bottleneck, and which one says a lot about where the IC architecture has gaps.

Curious which mandate is eating your time this quarter.
