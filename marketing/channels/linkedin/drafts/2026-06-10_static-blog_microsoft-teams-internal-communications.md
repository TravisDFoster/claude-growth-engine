## Caption

Microsoft is shipping a lot of AI into Teams in 2026. Copilot, intelligent recap, suggested replies, Loop components, Decisions. All real, all worth the licenses an enterprise is paying for.

It is also calibrated for the individual employee at their desk, not for the company-wide question of who needs to hear what, on which channel, in which language, and whether the message landed.

The new playbook walks through where Teams AI helps for internal communications, the four hard limits it hits (segmentation beyond AD groups, cross-channel read attribution, personalization at scale, orchestration), the integration patterns that extend it, and a 30-day plan to generate the data that funds the architecture upgrade.

The honest read: keep Teams as the collaboration spine. Layer the delivery and measurement system above it.
