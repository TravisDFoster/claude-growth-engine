## Slide 2 — See it, don't dig for it
Performance now reads as a picture before you have to read it as a number. A wider range of visuals surfaces trends, comparisons, and outliers at a glance.

Want the raw detail? Customizable tables let you add, remove, and rearrange columns, then export.

**Why it matters:** A quick visual read and a full data export now live on the same screen.

## Slide 3 — Navigation that gets out of your way
The old experience made deeper exploration feel like a maze. Insights reworked it from the ground up: breadcrumbs show where you are, and pathways stay consistent section to section.

Load times are faster too, so you stay in the question you were trying to answer.

**Why it matters:** You retrace your steps instead of starting over.

## Slide 4 — From the big picture to a single click
Start at a high-level summary, then drill into email and Blast engagement, content by channel, and audience detail down to how one person interacted with one message.

**Why it matters:** You are never stuck at the altitude the tool decided for you.

## Slide 5 — Every rate, compared to your own norm
A 22% open rate is a number floating in space until you know your baseline. Insights compares current performance against your organization's own average from the last 18 months.

**Why it matters:** That is the difference between a metric and an insight you can act on.

## Slide 6 — Dashboards you can actually share
Build custom dashboards from the blocks that matter, then decide who sees them. Admins get full visibility, team members share selectively, and permissions adjust as needs change.

**Why it matters:** Reporting moves from your screen into the conversation with leadership.
