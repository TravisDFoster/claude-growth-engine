## Caption

Someone asks how the all-staff update performed. How long does it take you to answer?

For a lot of internal comms teams, the honest answer is "longer than it should." You send the message, then dig through a reporting tool that was never built for a quick answer, clicking layers deep for the one number leadership wanted.

Insights changes that. It is a ground-up rebuild of the analytics inside Cerkl Broadcast, live for every Broadcast customer as of June 20, 2026.

The part that matters most for measurement: context. A 22% open rate on its own tells you nothing. Insights compares every rate against your own organization's average from the last 18 months, so you can see whether you landed above or below where your audience normally does. That is the difference between a metric and an insight.

You also get clearer visuals, faster load times, drill-down from a high-level summary to a single subscriber's click history, channel-level and News Digest reporting, and dashboards you can hand to a stakeholder without exporting a thing.

Communicators are measured on outcomes but rarely given time to be analysts. Insights closes that gap.

What's the one question about your comms you've been meaning to answer?
