# Template — LinkedIn Poll

> Light-asset post: the poll widget is the interaction. Caption sets up the question and the stakes; the poll captures the response.

## When to use this type
- Surfacing a question the audience already debates internally
- Quick temperature-check on a positioning hypothesis
- Audience-building (poll voters become reachable)

## Structure

**Caption (tight — 3–4 short paragraphs):**
- 1-line framing of why the question matters now
- 1–2 paragraphs of context or stakes
- A pointer to the poll

**Poll (the widget):**
- Question (concise, ≤140 chars)
- 2–4 answer options (LinkedIn max is 4)
- Duration: 1 week (Cerkl default)

## Skeleton

```markdown
# Poll — [Working title]

**Wraps:** [source pointer]
**Publish:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Owner:** [name]

## Caption

[Framing line]

[Context paragraph]

[Stakes paragraph]

[Pointer to the poll]

## Poll

**Question:** [≤140 chars]

**Options:**
1. [Option 1]
2. [Option 2]
3. [Option 3]
4. [Option 4]

**Duration:** 1 week
```
