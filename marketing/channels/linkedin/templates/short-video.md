# Template — LinkedIn Short Video

> Asset-heavy post: a 60-second video does the work, caption is short. Best for hook-driven takes that need a face and a voice.

## When to use this type
- A point that lands better said than written (tone, emphasis, energy)
- A founder / leader take with personality
- A 60-second walkthrough of a feature, stat, or argument

## Structure

**Caption (very short — 2–3 lines):**
- Hook line (specific, provocative, or counterintuitive)
- 1-line setup (optional)
- Downward pointer ("60-sec take ↓" / "Watch ↓")

**Asset (60-second video):**
- **0–3 sec — Hook:** Specific claim or visual that stops the scroll
- **3–50 sec — Body:** The argument. One point. No setup.
- **50–60 sec — Payoff:** Where to go next or one-line takeaway
