# Blog Pre-Writing Guide

In the folder [/Users/travisfoster/claude-code/cerkl/marketing/channels/organic/seo-blog/blog-posts-live], create a new .md file in naming format: YYYY-MM-DD_blog-post-title_draft.md

With the topic provided, fill out an outline and populate the properties below. These fields are used for categorization and publishing in Webflow. 

---

## Properties to Complete

- **Title**
- **Slug**
- **Top 3 Organic Search Keywords**
- **Primary Solution**
- **Top CTA**
- **Middle CTA**
- **Bottom CTA**
- **Primary Category**
- **Secondary Category**
- **Meta Description**
- **Meta Title** (typically the same as the Title)
- **Outline for Writing**

---

## Field Guidelines

### Primary Solution

Select the best option based on the topic and content:

| Option | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Omni AI** | Content covers multiple channels (email + Teams, mobile, etc.) or a channel that is not email |
| **Foundations** | Content covers only email or email-specific features (including email analytics) |
| **Educational** | All other blog post topics |

---

### CTA Guidelines

#### Top CTA

Leave blank when the Primary Solution is **Educational** or **Omni AI**.

| Option | When to Use |
|---|---|
| Email General Top | Article discusses both Gmail and Outlook, or is tool-agnostic |
| Gmail General Top | Article focuses specifically on Gmail |
| Outlook General Top | Article focuses specifically on Outlook |
| Nothing | Primary Solution is Educational or Omni AI |

#### Middle CTA

Leave blank when the Primary Solution is **Educational**.

| Option | When to Use |
|---|---|
| Email Design Middle | Content relates to designing emails |
| Email Distribution Lists Middle | Content relates to managing distribution lists |
| Email Surveys Middle | Content relates to email surveys |
| Email Analytics Middle | Content relates to email analytics or measurement |
| Email Newsletter Middle | Content relates to internal newsletters |
| Email Acknowledgments Middle | Content relates to email acknowledgments or read receipts |
| Omni AI Channel Complexity Middle | Omni AI content focused on managing multiple channels |
| Omni AI Deskless Workforce Middle | Omni AI content focused on frontline/deskless workers |
| Omni AI Personalization Middle | Omni AI content focused on personalization |
| Nothing | Primary Solution is Educational |

#### Bottom CTA

Always required — never left blank.

| Option | When to Use |
|---|---|
| Email General Bottom | Article discusses both Gmail and Outlook, or is tool-agnostic |
| Gmail General Bottom | Article focuses specifically on Gmail |
| Outlook General Bottom | Article focuses specifically on Outlook |

---

### Categories

#### Primary Category

Select the single best-fit category:

- Internal Email Communication
- Internal Communication Strategy
- Frontline and Mobile Workforce
- Internal Communications Measurement
- Employee Engagement and Experience

#### Secondary Category

Select a second category from the same list. Must be different from the Primary Category.
