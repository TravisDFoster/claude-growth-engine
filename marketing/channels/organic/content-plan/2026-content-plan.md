# 2026 Content Plan — May through December

## Overview

Marketing objective: Foundations subscriber growth. Every theme, topic, and campaign angle serves that goal — reaching HR managers, office managers, operations leads, and founders who are still running internal comms out of Outlook or Gmail.

Product launches shape two months directly: Insights Update in May and AI-assistant in July. The remaining months are anchored to seasonal ICP moments and ongoing Foundations positioning.

## May 2026 — Prove Your Impact

**Campaign Epic:** 2026 Insights Update

**Anchor:** Insights Update product launch

**ICP pain points**
- Sending emails blind — no way to know if anyone opened, clicked, or read
- No data to bring to leadership when justifying comms investment
- Open rates and engagement are guesswork without purpose-built analytics

**Key topics**
- What good internal comms analytics actually looks like
- How to benchmark open and click rates for employee email
- Using engagement data to improve messaging and targeting
- Building a case for comms investment using data (leadership-facing)
- Announcing the Insights Update — what's new, what it means for Foundations users

**Important dates**
- May 25 — Memorial Day
- Mental Health Awareness Month — angle: communicating employee wellness programs more effectively, using segmentation to reach the right audiences at the right time
- Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage Month — angle: communicating inclusively across diverse workforces

## December 2026 — Year in Review

- How to write a year-end message from leadership that employees actually read

- Setting up your 2027 internal comms calendar (template and framework)
- Why Q1 is the worst time to find a new comms tool — and what to do before January
- Foundations free plan: start before the new year, no credit card required

**Important dates**
- December 7 — Pearl Harbor Remembrance Day (light operational note if relevant to customer verticals like government or defense)
- December 24–25 — Christmas Eve and Christmas (content gap)
- December 31 — New Year's Eve (content gap)
- Light publishing cadence the last two weeks of the month; prioritize high-value evergreen content that works for January traffic
- Budget decision season: organizations finalizing 2027 tools budgets — strong moment for Foundations free plan messaging
