# Webinar Channel Context

## What we are building
Webinar program strategy and content for Cerkl Broadcast. Webinars serve dual purposes: demand generation for new Foundations subscribers and value delivery for existing users.

## Program notes
- Active webinar landing page is live
- On-demand recap content is produced post-event
- Collaborations with internal comms influencers and partners (e.g., Crescenzo Communications)
- Product and CS teams lead some webinars

## Topic strategy
Topics should map to problems the Foundations ICP is actively searching for help with — internal comms best practices, measurement, employee engagement, change communications. Avoid topics that are only relevant to paid (Omni AI) buyers unless the webinar is explicitly positioned for enterprise audiences.

## What good looks like
- Topics resonate with internal comms teams and HR/People leaders at SMBs and mid-market orgs
- On-demand recaps extend reach of each live event
- Each webinar produces at least one reusable content asset (blog recap, email, social clip)
- Registration is treated as a subscriber acquisition opportunity

## What to avoid
- Webinars that are product demos disguised as thought leadership
- Topics that only land for enterprise buyers (5,000+ employees)
- Events without a reuse plan for the content
