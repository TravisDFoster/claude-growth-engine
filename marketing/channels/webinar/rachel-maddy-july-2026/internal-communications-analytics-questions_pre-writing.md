## Outline

**Word count target**: 900–1,100 words
**Audience**: Foundations ICP — HR generalists / ops leads / whoever owns employee comms at 50–500 person companies. Pre-evaluation, not category-aware.
**CTA**: Webinar registration — https://us02web.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_-vu4-AcITfGJq0KEHysSSw

1. **Intro** — the gap between "we sent it" and "we can prove it worked." Lead with the named-employee proof point (you can name the one person on the leadership floor who never opened the reorg email), not an aggregate open rate.
2. **The five questions** — the spine of the post. Each is a question a communicator should be able to answer Monday morning:
   1. Did the right people actually read it? (reach + named read-attribution)
   2. Who didn't — and can I name them?
   3. Is engagement going up or down over time? (trend, not single-send noise)
   4. Which content actually changed behavior? (clicks/action, content effectiveness)
   5. Can I hand leadership the answer without building a deck? (data storytelling)
3. **Why this matters now** — leadership expects proof; the May 2026 Insights redesign makes these answerable in minutes.
4. **Meet the speakers** — Rachel Folz (product/measurement POV), Maddy Rieman (CS lens; bio pending).
5. **CTA** — register, date/time, what you'll see.
