## Clip 4 — We're not in the information age

### Editor block

- **Source timestamp:** 31:22 – 32:09 (~47s)
- **Speaker:** Tarek
- **Verbatim:**
  > "We're also not in the information age. People say that — we're not. We are in the attention age. People are receiving so much information that it just gets lost. The reason we are attracted to technologies like Amazon, Netflix, Hulu, Spotify is — all tailored to me, when I want it, about me, just me. That's how you break through. Sending one-to-many communication is difficult. It's a necessary evil — but there's also a way to make that stickier and resonate, and grab people's attention. Because you are in a battle for their attention."
