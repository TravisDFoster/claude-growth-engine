# Marketing Function Context

## Primary objective
Foundations subscriber growth. Converting free subscribers to paid (Foundations+ or Omni AI) is Product's responsibility. Marketing's job is to grow the number of active Foundations users.

## ICP for marketing
Broader than Cerkl's paid ICP. Because the goal is Foundations subscriber growth, SMB teams are in scope — not just mid-market/enterprise. The ICP is any internal comms team or people leader who would benefit from a better internal email workflow and is likely to find ongoing value in the free product.

Companies 0–500 employees are expected to stay on Foundations long-term. This is acceptable — subscriber growth is the goal, not conversion. Companies 501–2,000 are natural Foundations+ candidates; 2,001+ are natural Omni AI candidates. Conversion at those sizes is Product's responsibility.
