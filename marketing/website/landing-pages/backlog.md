## Scoring
- **Impact (1–10):** estimated effect on primary metric (CTA click-through). 10 = page-changing.
- **Confidence (1–10):** evidence behind the hypothesis. Data > customer research > competitor pattern > gut.
- **Ease (1–10):** time + effort to ship the test. 10 = copy-only swap on a flag, ready today.
- **ICE = (Impact + Confidence + Ease) / 3**
