**Layer relationship:** `cerkl-research/` is the **durable knowledge layer** (hand-curated wiki, source-provenance enforced). `ic-trends/` and `competitor-marketing/` are the **ephemeral signal layer** (horizon-scan, dated artifacts). Signal can be ingested *into* the vault when worth keeping (Travis points; vault's CLAUDE.md governs the ingest). The vault **only writes inside `cerkl-research/wiki/`** — it reads from sibling folders for context but never modifies them.

HTML rendering uses the cross-workspace skill: [`cerkl/skills/md-to-html/`](../skills/md-to-html/SKILL.md) (dispatched as a sub-agent per process Step 5).

## Conventions

- **Output file naming**: `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md` (absolute date, no relative phrases).
- **Citations**: every claim has a URL inline.

- Surface, don't summarize — Travis reads originals when worth it; the recap exists to triage
