# IC Deep Dive — Haiilo

**Date:** 2026-05-10   |   **Category:** Competitor

## TL;DR

Haiilo is the post-merger employee-comms platform formed in 2022 from COYO (Germany, social intranet), Smarp (Finland, employee advocacy), and Jubiwee (France, people analytics) — now leaning on an "AI-powered intranet + employee advocacy + insights in one platform" pitch built around the AVA AI assistant. Distinctive vs. the rest of Cerkl's competitive set because of the *employee-advocacy/social-sharing* module (the Smarp lineage) and a heavy EU footprint; weaker than peers on personalization, audience segmentation, structured governance, and analytics depth — exactly the gaps a 2,000–12,000 employee Cerkl Omni AI buyer cares about. Cerkl does not have a live "vs. Haiilo" SEO page and should commission one.

## What it is

Haiilo is a Hamburg, Germany-headquartered employee communications and employee experience platform created by the 2022 merger/rebrand of three companies: COYO (Hamburg, founded 2010 by Jan Marquardt and Marko Ilic), Smarp (Helsinki, employee advocacy), and Jubiwee (Paris, people analytics / engagement surveys) ([Haiilo brand-launch blog](https://blog.haiilo.com/blog/haiilo-brand-launch/); [Business Wire merger announcement, 2022-02-23](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220223005971/en)). Backed by Marlin Equity Partners (acquired 2020, pre-merger) and Nauta Capital; ~$33M total funding ([Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/companies/haiilo/__kJwngNW74BveFiUcHbGSIBSo5DbcVEgfKXXoFRma6bM); [Crunchbase acquisition profile](https://www.crunchbase.com/acquisition/marlin-equity-partners-acquires-coyo--a0e78841)). ~300 employees across Atlanta, London, Hamburg, Berlin, Paris and Helsinki ([Haiilo about page via Yoyaba case study](https://www.yoyaba.com/en/success-story/haiilo)). Positioned today as an "Employee Communications & Intranet Software" platform combining intranet, multichannel comms, employee advocacy, and engagement insights ([haiilo.com homepage, per G2 vendor page](https://www.g2.com/products/haiilo-haiilo/reviews); [Haiilo platform overview](https://www.haiilo.com/platform-overview)).

## What it does / claims

Haiilo sells a unified suite that bundles three historically separate products under one brand:

- **Employee Communications / Intranet (COYO lineage)** — social-feed intranet, multichannel publishing (web, mobile app, email), communities and interest groups, 135+ integrations, customizable layouts ([Haiilo platform overview](https://www.haiilo.com/platform-overview); [Capterra product page](https://www.capterra.com/p/251791/Haiilo/)).
- **Employee Advocacy / Haiilo Amplify (Smarp lineage)** — standalone module letting employees connect personal LinkedIn/Facebook/X accounts and share company content externally, with leaderboards, points, and analytics on reach/clicks/engagement ([Haiilo employee advocacy page](https://www.haiilo.com/employee-advocacy); [Smarp-is-now-Haiilo page](https://www.haiilo.com/smarp); [Haiilo Amplify on App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/haiilo-amplify/id919085537)). The headline customer case study claims 2,033% ROI from Salesforce's employee advocacy program built on Haiilo ([customer stories](https://www.haiilo.com/customer-stories)).
- **Insights / Engagement (Jubiwee lineage)** — engagement surveys and analytics, originally a separate French SaaS now folded in ([Business Wire, 2022-02-23](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220223005971/en)).
- **Haiilo AVA (AI assistant)** — content drafting, summarization, hashtag suggestion, in-search Q&A grounded in platform content, event cover-image generation, and AI-generated social sharing messages for advocates. Customers can plug in OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, or Google Vertex AI, or use Haiilo's turnkey AI ([Haiilo AI features](https://www.haiilo.com/ai-features); [AVA overview, Support Center](https://support.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/20568257379730-Haiilo-AVA-overview); [AVA OpenAI/Azure/Vertex setup](https://advocacy-knowledgebase.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/22269328598930-Setting-up-AVA-with-OpenAI-Azure-OpenAI-or-Google-Vertex-AI)).

Shipping cadence (Q3 2025 → Q2 2026) — note this is partial; the detailed roadmap is gated to the customer community at community.haiilo.com ([release notes article](https://support.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016573720-Release-notes-product-updates-and-the-roadmap)):
- 2025: AVA rolled out across Studio (content authoring), search, and Amplify (advocate sharing messages) ([AVA in Studio](https://support.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/12109080276626-Using-AVA-in-the-Studio); [AI sharing messages](https://advocacy-knowledgebase.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/16478712162962-Generating-AI-sharing-messages)).
- 2025: User sync with HR systems (Workday, SageHR, Personio) for automatic account provisioning ([release-notes summary via search](https://support.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016573720-Release-notes-product-updates-and-the-roadmap)).
- 2025: Content calendar for channel managers to track scheduled posts.
- Mid-2025: New plugin-adapter certificate requirements (security tightening for plug-in developers, effective 2025-06-01) ([plugin changelog](https://dev.plugins.haiilo.com/changelog?page=2)).
- Q1–Q2 2026: Monthly "Your Product Update in [month]" cadence on the blog ([Feb update](https://blog.haiilo.com/blog/your-product-update-in-february/); [March update](https://blog.haiilo.com/blog/your-product-update-in-march/)) — no major net-new module announced publicly; iteration on AVA and intranet UX.

**Post-merger product story to flag:** Haiilo is still visibly three products with one brand. The Employee Advocacy module ships as a *standalone* product separate from the comms suite ([Sociabble Haiilo alternatives roundup](https://www.sociabble.com/blog/comparison/best-haiilo-alternatives/)). The intranet, advocacy, and insights modules each have their own knowledge bases and support paths (`support.haiilo.com` vs. `advocacy-knowledgebase.haiilo.com`). This is the seam Cerkl can press on.

## Who it's for

- **Primary user / ICP overlap:** EU-headquartered mid-market and enterprise organizations (2,000+ employees) where (a) the buyer wants a unified comms + intranet platform *and* (b) employee advocacy / external social amplification is a stated goal. Strong industries: large transportation (Deutsche Bahn), telecom (Deutsche Telekom), healthcare (Asklepios), CPG (Ritter Sport), professional services (KPMG) ([Haiilo customer logos, via Yoyaba](https://www.yoyaba.com/en/success-story/haiilo)). Plans typically start at 500 user licenses ([G2 pricing page](https://www.g2.com/products/haiilo-haiilo/pricing)).
- **Strong fit when:** Buyer wants intranet + advocacy bundled; marketing/comms wants employees as a distribution channel for external content; EU data residency / German-engineering preference is a procurement factor; social-feed UX is preferred over structured-publishing UX.
- **Weak fit when:** Buyer wants deep audience segmentation and personalization (per LumApps' own comparison: "Haiilo lacks audience segmentation, making targeted communication impossible" ([LumApps vs Haiilo](https://www.lumapps.com/insights/blog/haiilo-vs-lumapps))); compliance-heavy or governance-heavy environment; org wants a delivery + measurement layer on top of existing channels rather than a new home base; sub-500 employees; no appetite for employee advocacy as a use case.

## How it slots into Cerkl's competitive set / positioning

Haiilo is already in the main competitor list in `shared/competitors.md` but has *no* live "vs. Haiilo" SEO page and no head-to-head positioning paragraph. That's a gap worth closing — Haiilo is structurally different from Staffbase/Simpplr/LumApps and Cerkl's existing competitive narrative does not address it cleanly.

1. **Cerkl's core counter-frame still applies — and is sharper against Haiilo than against Staffbase.** *"We're the delivery + measurement layer; the intranet is the destination, not the comms strategy."* Haiilo is *more* intranet-as-destination than Staffbase, because Haiilo's center of gravity is a social-feed home base where employees go to read, react, and share. If the buyer's actual job is "make sure the right message reaches every employee through whatever channel they actually open," Haiilo is the wrong architecture — it presumes employees come to Haiilo. Cerkl's pitch (sync audiences from your HRIS, deliver into Outlook/Gmail/Teams/Slack/intranet you already own, measure cross-channel) is the inverse.

2. **The employee-advocacy angle is a real differentiator for Haiilo — and is *not* a threat to Cerkl, because Cerkl doesn't play there.** Employee advocacy (employees sharing company content to *external* social networks) is a marketing-team motion, not an internal-comms motion. It's the Smarp lineage and Haiilo's most distinctive surface area vs. Staffbase/Simpplr/LumApps/Firstup ([Sociabble: Haiilo alternatives](https://www.sociabble.com/blog/comparison/best-haiilo-alternatives/)). For Cerkl this is a *dilution risk for Haiilo*, not a strength: when Haiilo shows up in a Cerkl deal, the IC buyer is being sold a tool whose ROI case study (the 2,033% Salesforce number) is a *marketing program* metric, not an internal-comms metric. AEs can use that. The counter-line: *"Haiilo's flagship customer story is a marketing-team ROI number. We're an internal-comms platform — we measure whether your employees actually read the message."*

3. **ICP fit is narrower than it looks.** Haiilo's customer base skews EU-headquartered enterprise (Deutsche Bahn, Deutsche Telekom, Ritter Sport, KPMG-DE, Asklepios) with 500+ user minimums and German-engineering provenance. Cerkl's paid ICP (2,000–12,200+ employees, US-anchored, distributed/frontline/deskless) overlaps in headcount but not in geography or buying motion. Where it *does* overlap is mid-market US orgs with EU operations — and there, Haiilo's documented weaknesses (no audience segmentation, limited personalization, weaker governance per [LumApps comparison](https://www.lumapps.com/insights/blog/haiilo-vs-lumapps); structural rigidity and customization limits per [Sociabble's Haiilo roundup](https://www.sociabble.com/blog/comparison/best-haiilo-alternatives/)) are exactly the things Omni AI's segmentation engine is built for.

4. **Cerkl should commission a "vs. Haiilo" SEO page.** It's missing from the live set (Simpplr, LumApps, Firstup, Workvivo, Poppulo are live per `shared/competitors.md`), Haiilo is in the main competitor list, and the head-to-head story writes itself: (a) we're the delivery layer, they're a destination; (b) we have audience segmentation, they don't (per their competitors' own comparison pages); (c) we don't replace your existing intranet — and we're not asking you to bolt on an external-advocacy program you didn't ask for; (d) we have a free Foundations entry point and self-serve onboarding vs. their 500-seat minimum and sales-led custom pricing.

   **AE counter-positioning lines:**
   - *"Haiilo is three products in a trench coat — intranet, advocacy, and surveys merged in 2022. We're one product built for one job: getting your internal message read, on the channels your employees already use."*
   - *"Their flagship ROI case study is Salesforce's marketing-team employee advocacy program. If your KPI is internal message read-through, not external social reach, you're being sold a different tool."*
   - *"Haiilo has no audience segmentation — their own competitors point that out, and reviews on G2 confirm it. Omni AI's segmentation engine is exactly the wedge."*
   - *"500-seat minimum, custom quote, sales-led. Cerkl Foundations is free and live in an afternoon."*

## Gotchas

- **Three-product seams from the COYO + Smarp + Jubiwee merger are still visible four years in.** Employee Advocacy ships as a standalone module on a separate knowledge base (`advocacy-knowledgebase.haiilo.com`) from the main intranet (`support.haiilo.com`). Buyers expecting a fully unified product may be surprised.
- **Audience segmentation gap.** Competitors (LumApps, Sociabble) and review-aggregator summaries consistently call out limited segmentation and personalization vs. peers ([LumApps vs Haiilo](https://www.lumapps.com/insights/blog/haiilo-vs-lumapps); [Sociabble Haiilo roundup](https://www.sociabble.com/blog/comparison/best-haiilo-alternatives/)). For Cerkl's Omni AI ICP, this is the most important functional gap.
- **No public pricing; 500-seat minimum.** Custom quote-driven sales motion; no free trial; no free plan ([G2 pricing](https://www.g2.com/products/haiilo-haiilo/pricing); [TrustRadius pricing](https://www.trustradius.com/products/haiilo/pricing); [Connecteam Haiilo review](https://connecteam.com/reviews/haiilo/)). Multiple Capterra reviewers cite pricing as a deal-breaker.
- **Employee-advocacy fit-or-not-for-buyer.** The Salesforce 2,033% ROI case study and the Amplify module are external-social-sharing plays. If the IC buyer doesn't have a marketing-led advocacy program (or doesn't want one), they're paying for a module they won't use.
- **Governance / compliance is light.** Haiilo functions as a communications + social hub more than a structured-governance intranet ([LumApps vs Haiilo](https://www.lumapps.com/insights/blog/haiilo-vs-lumapps)). Compliance-heavy buyers (healthcare, finance, regulated manufacturing) will gravitate to Staffbase or LumApps.
- **No surfaced Gartner Magic Quadrant or Forrester Wave placement** in 2025–2026 searches — Haiilo has Gartner Peer Insights presence but did not appear as a named leader/challenger in the analyst placements I searched. May simply be a search-coverage gap; worth verifying with a direct source if it ever matters in a deal.
- **Slack integration reported broken** by at least one Capterra reviewer who chose the platform specifically for that ([Capterra reviews](https://www.capterra.com/p/251791/Haiilo/reviews/)).
- **AVA is provider-agnostic but not zero-config.** Customers either pay Haiilo for turnkey AI or bring their own OpenAI/Azure/Vertex keys ([AVA setup docs](https://advocacy-knowledgebase.haiilo.com/hc/en-us/articles/22269328598930-Setting-up-AVA-with-OpenAI-Azure-OpenAI-or-Google-Vertex-AI)). That's flexible but also means IT is in the AI deployment loop.
