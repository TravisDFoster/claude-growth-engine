01This week in one paragraph

After a month of near-weekly hero churn, the positioning layer went quiet — zero H1 swaps among reachable vendors, and Staffbase held its "one platform" H1 for a second straight week (the A/B testing looks settled). The action moved to the content/proof layer, and the axis rotated: frontline/deskless is the new gated-research battleground. Firstup dropped a brand-new report — "Communications crisis in manufacturing: what 1,000 frontline workers revealed" (06-30) — and Workvivo continues featuring its "Frontline AI Gap" report, shifting the substantiation contest away from generic "prove IC value" toward "reach the deskless worker." That maps straight onto Cerkl's Omni AI ICP and is corroborated by this week's daily-trend signal (two deskless case studies: Sav-A-Tree, Goodwill). Meanwhile the measurement hook grew again to 8/10 ad creative (now the category default), shadow-AI fear vanished from the hero layer (Staffbase's 79%/69% stat dropped off), and Staffbase turned conquester — surging to a 7/10 SERP-presence tie with ContactMonkey and shipping "Simpplr Alternatives" + "ContactMonkey Alternatives" pages. Free-forever holds at 0/10 (week 9), and no new mid-market (2K–10K) logo shipped anywhere this window — the contested zone is quiet and open.
