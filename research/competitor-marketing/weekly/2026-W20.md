## Pain-point cross-cut

> The 2-3 buyer problems competitors are leading with this week, derived across positioning + ads + content.

### Pain point 1: "We launched AI but can't see outcomes" — the AI execution gap
- **Who's claiming it:** Staffbase (VOICES keynote framing), LumApps (new H1), Poppulo (two posts in 3 days), ContactMonkey (Cates' "execution problem" quote), Firstup (AI as top-nav).
- **How they're framing it:**
  - Staffbase / Frank Wolf VOICES keynote: "past 'should we?' and into 'how do we?'" with the McKinsey-style "90% launched AI, 5% see outcomes" stat. ([press](https://staffbase.com/press/releases/voices-2026-brings-insights-on-the-human-edge-in-the-ai-era))
  - LumApps new H1: *"The leading AI Employee Hub"* + "where work comes together — connecting people, tools, AI agents, and knowledge." ([lumapps.com](https://www.lumapps.com/))
  - Poppulo: *"How AI and Alignment Could Make or Break HR and Comms"* ([2026-05-13 post](https://www.poppulo.com/employee-experience-platform/blog/ai-alignment-hr-comms)).
  - ContactMonkey / Cates: *"…they really have is an execution problem… build the system that closes that gap."* ([news](https://www.contactmonkey.com/news/jeff-cates-as-ceo))
- **Cerkl's current angle:** Cerkl's positioning ("the delivery + measurement layer across the channels you already have") engages this *implicitly* — but Cerkl is not visibly leading with "we close the AI execution gap" messaging. Omni AI exists but isn't framed as the execution layer.
- **Implication:** **Lean in, but with a sharper frame.** The category is moving from "we have AI" to "we make AI deliver." Cerkl's product story (right message, right person, right channel, right time) is literally the execution layer for AI-generated content — it can and should make that explicit. Suggested angle: *"AI writes the message. Cerkl makes sure the right employee actually reads it."*

### Pain point 2: "How do I prove IC impact?" — measurement / metrics anxiety
- **Who's claiming it:** Poppulo, AxiosHQ, ContactMonkey, Firstup, Workvivo (Seer launch). Same set as 2026-05-11, but Poppulo's continued cadence (third measurement-adjacent post in 8 days) hardens the trend.
- **How they're framing it:** Unchanged from Monday — Workvivo's 62%/49%/42%/39% feedback-action gap stats ([Seer LP](https://www.workvivo.com/seer/)) are now VERIFIED as the most-quantified pain claim in the category.
- **Cerkl's current angle:** `shared/competitors.md` lists "cross-channel measurement" as a core differentiator. The opportunity from Monday stands: publish a POV that contrasts dashboard-style measurement with delivery-level personalization metrics across channels.
- **Implication:** Lean in. See "Ideas to steal" #2.

### Pain point 3: "The competitor in your stack is the wrong choice" — direct conquest SEO
- **Who's claiming it:** Staffbase (vs Firstup + Switch from Firstup), LumApps (Workvivo alternatives + Simpplr alternatives), plus third-party comparison-site noise (Blink, Jostle, MangoApps).
- **How they're framing it:** Direct head-to-head pages targeting "alternatives" and "vs" intent. Staffbase's "Switch from Firstup" is the most aggressive — it's a *migration* CTA, not a comparison.
- **Cerkl's current angle:** `shared/competitors.md` lists 5 live "vs." pages (Simpplr, LumApps, Firstup, Workvivo, Poppulo) and 3 gaps (Haiilo, Axios HQ, Workshop). No "Switch from X" pages exist.
- **Implication:** **Counter-attack with a product-led wedge.** Cerkl can't out-spend on conquest SEO, but it can publish "Switch from [Workshop / AxiosHQ / ContactMonkey] in an afternoon" pages that *include the free-tier on-ramp*. No competitor can match "switch in an afternoon, free forever" because none of them offer free.

## Ideas to steal

- **Conquest "Switch from X" pages with free-tier on-ramp.** Staffbase's [Switch from Firstup](https://staffbase.com/switch-from-firstup) is a strong template — it converts comparison intent into migration intent.
  - *How Cerkl might use this:* Build "Switch from Workshop / AxiosHQ / ContactMonkey" pages framed around *"Foundations is free forever. Migrate in an afternoon."* No competitor can match this, and these are the three competitors most exposed at the Foundations tier.

- **Quantified-gap creative + gated report + webinar funnel.** Workvivo's [Seer launch](https://www.workvivo.com/seer/) is the cleanest one-week funnel in the set: stat ("62% comfortable / 49% see change") → gated report → webinar (5/20) → in-person event (5/21).
  - *How Cerkl might use this:* Build a "Cross-Channel Read Gap" report (Cerkl's only-source data — what % of intended audience actually reads a given comms across email/intranet/Teams/Slack) → gate it → tie to a webinar. The stat is Cerkl's structural advantage; competitors with single-channel data can't produce it.

- **Material H1 swap as a marketing event, not a rebrand.** LumApps changed its category claim in a week (from soft brand line to "The leading AI Employee Hub") and presumably ran no announcement around it.
  - *How Cerkl might use this:* Cerkl can iterate hero copy aggressively to test the "AI execution layer" message without it being framed as a rebrand. A/B the H1, measure activation lift on Foundations sign-ups.

- **CEO transition narrative.** Cates' first quote at ContactMonkey is a textbook category-redefinition move ([news](https://www.contactmonkey.com/news/jeff-cates-as-ceo)). Even if Cerkl isn't in transition, the *form* — a leader putting a sharp word on what the buyer actually has wrong — is a useful template for thought-leadership posts.
  - *How Cerkl might use this:* Tracy or a Cerkl exec publishes a sharp "What internal comms teams actually have wrong" piece. Land an opinion, then point at the product.

- **Vertical-week wedge (continued from last week).** Firstup added a third frontline-healthcare post this week ([Florence Nightingale post](https://firstup.io/blog/nurse-communication-gap-healthcare-2026/)) — still no other vendor playing in vertical content.
  - *How Cerkl might use this:* Same recommendation as Monday — build a 2026 calendar of "industry weeks" and publish a Cerkl-customer story + Foundations on-ramp for each. The vacuum is still there.
