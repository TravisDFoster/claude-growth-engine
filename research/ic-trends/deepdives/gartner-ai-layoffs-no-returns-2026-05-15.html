05Gotchas

Sample skew
350 executives, ≥$1B revenue, all already piloting autonomous business. Not the SMB or mid-market story — it's enterprise. Don't overstate to a Foundations buyer.
Self-reported attribution
"Workforce reduction tied to AI initiatives" is self-reported. Some are real AI-driven; some are AI-washed (per Sam Altman's February 2026 acknowledgment of "AI washing where people are blaming AI for layoffs they would otherwise do"). Gartner doesn't separate these.
Forecast vs. data
The "net positive jobs by 2028–2029" prediction is forecast, not data. Cite the empirical finding (no correlation between cuts and ROI), not the optimistic forecast — the forecast is contested and aging quickly.
Paywall reality
Gartner press releases are funnel content for paid research. The full underlying report is behind the paywall. Buyers can cite the press release but not the methodology in depth. Plan citations accordingly.
Wide cut range
The 1–15% workforce reduction range is wide. A 1% cut and a 15% cut are very different stories; the press release flattens them.
