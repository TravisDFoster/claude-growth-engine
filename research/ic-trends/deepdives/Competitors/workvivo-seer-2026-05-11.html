01What it is & what it does

What it is

Seer is Workvivo's new people-intelligence offering, launched 2026-05-06 via a dedicated product page at workvivo.com/seer and a press release on Workvivo's newsroom (Workvivo newsroom, GlobeNewswire).

It is "available as a standalone solution or integrated into the broader Workvivo platform" — meaning Workvivo is selling Seer outside its own install base, not just to existing customers. Workvivo is owned by Zoom (acquired April 2023).

Seer is built in-house, not acquired, and is staffed by an ex-Glint/Qualtrics leadership team:

  • Justin Black — Head of Seer; former head of Glint at Microsoft/LinkedIn, founder of Glint's People Science team
  • Phil Murphy — Head of Growth, ex-Qualtrics
  • Jaime Gonzales — Principal People Scientist, ex-Microsoft/LinkedIn/Glint

Source URLs: product page · press release · UC Today · intro video. Pricing is gated — no public floor or SKU.

What it does / claims

Seer "combines feedback with real-time signals from communication, collaboration, and engagement" (press release). Concretely, the buyer gets:

  • Listening surface — customizable engagement surveys, pulse surveys, polls/quizzes spanning HQ and frontline.
  • AI analysis — automated theme detection and risk surfacing across thousands of open-ended comments.
  • Segmentation — by region, team, tenure.
  • Auto-generated executive reports for the people-strategy audience.
  • AI-powered manager dashboards — personalized, mobile-first, with guided next steps for line managers.
  • In-product action tools — post updates, launch initiatives, create spaces directly from Seer (the differentiator vs. Glint/Culture Amp/Peakon, which require leaving the listening tool to act).
  • Progress tracking on implemented changes — closing the feedback-to-outcome loop.

02Who it's for

Primary user / ICP

Mid-market and enterprise HR / people / EX leaders evaluating an employee-listening platform — not internal-comms managers. Manager-level use case is the secondary persona (manager-action dashboards).

Strong fit when

Buyer already runs Workvivo and/or Zoom Workplace; wants action-in-product rather than survey-and-export; is dissatisfied with Glint cycle-based listening; values an ex-Glint pedigree in the vendor team.

Weak fit when

HRIS gravity is Workday (Peakon's stronger fit); buyer needs heavyweight statistical/predictive modeling (Qualtrics); wants engagement + performance + development in one suite (Culture Amp); or is purely evaluating IC delivery tooling (Cerkl's lane, not Seer's).

05Gotchas

"Standalone" but Zoom/Workvivo-flavored

Workvivo positions Seer as standalone, but the differentiation story ("real-time signals from communication, collaboration, and engagement") leans heavily on passive telemetry from Workvivo and Zoom Workplace. For a buyer without either, the in-product action layer and the manager-dashboard guidance lose some of their differentiation against Glint/Culture Amp.

Sales-led with custom quotes — buyers should expect Workvivo's procurement profile (~$20k/year floor as of 2026 per the existing Workvivo deep-dive). No published Seer-specific SKU or floor.
