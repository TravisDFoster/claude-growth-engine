# IC Trends — 2026-05-11

**Today's headline takeaway:** The IC conversation around AI flipped from adoption to anxiety this week — practitioners are calling out "AI slop," demanding vendor-incident readiness, and watching a bifurcated hiring market (Anthropic/OpenAI paying $1M for comms hires while ~1/3 of ICs say AI is the stated reason for headcount cuts at their org). On the vendor side, Forrester Wave Intranet Platforms Q2 2026 anointed Simpplr/Workvivo/Unily/LumApps as Leaders, Workvivo formally launched **Seer** people-intelligence, and Poppulo, ContactMonkey, Staffbase, and Firstup all dropped POV content positioning for the next cycle.
