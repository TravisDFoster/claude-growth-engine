# IC Trends — 2026-07-01

**Today's headline takeaway:** The week's signal converges hard on one thesis: **AI adoption is failing as a communications problem, and IC owns the fix.** SHRM's new data shows only 33% of individual contributors were told about their org's AI adoption vs. 74% of directors; Karian's IC Index says just 35% of employees think their org applies AI to the right problems and 32% got clear guidance; McKinsey pegs fully-scaled AI at 7%; HBR flags middle managers as the overloaded bottleneck; and Forrester explicitly reframes IC as the strategic lever for EX. This is a cleaner, more ownable narrative for Cerkl than yesterday's benchmark-report arms race.

## Top items

### 1. SHRM: the AI-adoption comms gap, quantified — 33% of ICs were told about AI adoption vs. 74% of directors
**Source:** SHRM   |   **URL:** https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/research/navigating-ai-in-the-workplace/full-report   |   **Date:** 2026-06-15   |   **Category:** Analyst
SHRM's *Navigating AI in the Workplace 2026* finds a stark information asymmetry — leadership knows the AI plan, the frontline doesn't — and names that comms gap as a primary reason rollouts stall.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** The single strongest data point this week for the "AI adoption is a delivery-and-reach problem" pitch. Corroborated by Karian (item 5) — two independent datasets saying the same thing in one week. Cerkl's wedge: the plan doesn't matter if it never reaches (or gets read by) the people expected to adopt it.

### 2. Forrester: make internal communications a strategic lever for employee (and brand) experience
**Source:** Forrester   |   **URL:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/make-internal-communications-a-strategic-lever-for-employee-and-brand-experience/   |   **Date:** 2026-06   |   **Category:** Analyst

**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Analyst air-cover for elevating IC from cost center to strategic function — exactly the budget narrative Cerkl's buyers use internally. Pull for Foundations+ / Omni AI upmarket content.

### 3. McKinsey: are your people ready for AI at scale? (only 7% are)
**Source:** McKinsey   |   **URL:** https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/people-and-organizational-performance/our-insights/the-organization-blog/are-your-people-ready-for-ai-at-scale   |   **Date:** 2026-06-08   |   **Category:** Analyst

**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Reinforces items 1–2 — the bottleneck is sustained, targeted internal comms, not tooling. Feeds the same adoption-gap story.

### 4. HBR: AI adoption is overloading your middle managers

**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Connects the manager-engagement thread (covered 06-17) to the AI-adoption thread. The implied fix — reach and equip managers directly, at scale — is a targeting/segmentation use case squarely in Cerkl's lane.
