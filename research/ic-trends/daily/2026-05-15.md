# IC Trends — 2026-05-15

**Today's headline takeaway:** Gartner stacked two major releases this week pushing back on the "AI replaces workers" narrative — the headline stat: 19% of employees report *zero* time saved from AI. At the same time, Simpplr's fresh State-of-IC follow-up quantifies the scope-creep practitioners keep flagging: only 27% of IC teams expect to add headcount in 2026 (down from 38%) even as AI/governance scope balloons.
