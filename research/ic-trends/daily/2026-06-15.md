# IC Trends — 2026-06-15

**Today's headline takeaway:** The week's IC-publication signal converges on one move — Ragan and PR Daily are formalizing the **"engagement → experience" pivot** (two pushes, 06-11 and 06-15), while the practical conversation has shifted to **IC owning AI adoption** (NY Life's "9 lessons," Amazon's AI-leaderboard misfire). On the competitive side it's a positioning week, not a product week: Staffbase is pressing bottom-funnel frontline-tools SEO and Poppulo is claiming "undisputed market leader" after the Sociabble deal.

## Top items

### 1. The "engagement → experience" pivot goes from theme to mandate
**Source:** Ragan / PR Daily   |   **URL:** https://www.ragan.com/why-communicators-are-trading-employee-engagement-for-employee-experience/ · https://www.ragan.com/your-input-needed-the-new-employee-experience-mandate/   |   **Date:** 2026-06-11 / 2026-06-15   |   **Category:** Publication
Ragan ran "Why communicators are trading employee engagement for employee experience" (06-11), then launched a "New Employee Experience Mandate" reader survey (06-15) — the trade press is now actively reframing the field's success metric from clicks/opens/engagement toward lived experience and business outcomes, especially for frontline.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** This is the same vocabulary the competitor set is racing toward (Workvivo "EXP," LumApps "AI Employee Hub," Poppulo "EX platform"). Cerkl's measurement story — delivered/opened/read by employee — is the concrete proof layer under the abstract "experience" narrative; lean into it before "experience" becomes a feeds-and-intranet land grab.

### 2. Ragan: "9 lessons from building an AI-integrated comms function" (NY Life CCO)
**Source:** Ragan   |   **URL:** https://www.ragan.com/9-lessons-from-building-an-ai-integrated-comms-function/   |   **Date:** 2026-06-11   |   **Category:** Publication
A sitting CCO's practical playbook for embedding AI into the comms function — operating model, not hype. Continues the multi-week thread of IC owning AI adoption/change rather than consuming AI features.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** The buyer conversation is maturing from "does it have AI" to "how do I run an AI-integrated function." Cerkl's wedge is delivery + measurement of those AI-assisted messages, not another assistant — content useful for the IC-owns-AI narrative.

### 3. Ragan: Amazon's AI-leaderboard headache shows why adoption signals matter
