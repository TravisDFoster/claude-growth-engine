### 3. Gartner: AI layoffs create budget room but no ROI
**Source:** Gartner   |   **URL:** https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2026-05-05-gartner-says-autonomous-business-and-artificial-intelligence-layoffs-may-create-budget-room-but-do-not-deliver-returns   |   **Date:** 2026-05-05   |   **Category:** Analyst
Of orgs deploying autonomous-business / AI tech, **~80% cut headcount, but ROI was statistically equal between cutters and non-cutters.** Returns came from investing in the people who orchestrate the AI, not from cutting the people the AI replaced.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Hard data that contradicts the season's CEO-memo trend (Cloudflare "doesn't need measurers," Wix "directly due to AI," Cisco "AI-driven restructuring," Intuit's denial). Useful for Cerkl thought-leadership content arguing that IC's job in 2026 is not to deliver the layoff memo — it's to prove the productivity case for the people who remain. The numbers anchor the argument.
