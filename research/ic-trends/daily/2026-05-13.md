# IC Trends — 2026-05-13

**Today's headline takeaway:** Today's signal converges on one theme: AI-era IC is being reframed from a *tooling* story into a *trust and landing* story. Forrester now positions IC as a strategic EX/brand lever (not a push channel), Staffbase and Poppulo both shipped fresh "AI trust/alignment" POV pieces today, and PR Daily named the widening gap between *sent* and *landed* that AI content volume is making worse — squarely Cerkl's lane.
