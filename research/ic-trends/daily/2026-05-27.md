## Top items

### 1. Poppulo acquires Sociabble — adds advocacy, frontline mobile, and rewards to its EX platform
**Source:** Poppulo   |   **URL:** https://www.poppulo.com/employee-experience-platform/blog/poppulo-acquires-sociabble   |   **Date:** 2026-05-26   |   **Category:** Competitor
Vista-backed Poppulo absorbs Sociabble (~100 staff, ~50M employee reach), bolting social intranet, frontline mobile, employee advocacy, and rewards onto its EX platform. Signals an explicit pivot from "digital signage + comms" to multi-product employee-experience suite.

Cerkl's "we're the delivery + measurement layer across the channels you already have" reframe still holds, but the Poppulo vs. Cerkl SEO refresh should now address EX-suite-positioning, not just AI editor parity.

### 3. Gartner Predicts 2026: 75% of employees will use chatbots over intranets/newsletters/managers by 2028
**Source:** Gartner (paywalled — anchor via Ragan)   |   **URL:** https://www.ragan.com/ai-hr-comms-gartner-2026/   |   **Date:** 2026-05 (recent publication of 2026 predictions)   |   **Category:** Analyst

**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Direct validation of the Cerkl reframe: "the intranet is the destination, not the comms strategy." Worth lifting the chatbot stat into vs. Staffbase / Simpplr / LumApps positioning copy — those vendors are betting on intranet-as-content-source AI, while Gartner is forecasting intranets are not where employees will go for info.

### 4. HBR: Managers Are Struggling to Keep Up with the AI Productivity Boom
**Source:** HBR   |   **URL:** https://hbr.org/2026/05/managers-are-struggling-to-keep-up-with-the-ai-productivity-boom   |   **Date:** 2026-05-25   |   **Category:** Analyst
AI lets individual contributors ship faster than legacy review/management cadences can absorb — managers become a bottleneck on AI-driven output. The fix is new operating rhythms, not more meetings.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Pairs with the rolling-7day Gallup finding (manager support = 8.7x AI multiplier) — managers are the structural choke point of internal AI rollout. Cerkl's manager-cascade angle (right message, right person, right channel) is a defensible counter-narrative to "we'll fix it with another intranet AI."
