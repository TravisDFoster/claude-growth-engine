### 1. Simpplr launches AI Control Center — governance-first IT play
**Source:** Simpplr blog   |   **URL:** https://www.simpplr.com/blog/ai-control-center/   |   **Date:** 2026-05-25   |   **Category:** Competitor
Lets IT admins enable/disable individual AI features, pick the underlying LLM (Azure OpenAI or Google Gemini), and maintain audit logs across the platform. The pitch is anchored to a Forrester stat — "78% of IT leaders say they need better security frameworks to scale AI safely" — positioning Simpplr as the safe choice when IT becomes the co-buyer.
**Why it matters for Cerkl:** Simpplr is layering a *governance* surface on top of its Feb 2026 Comms AI orchestration push — the same direction Staffbase is taking with Navigator + AI Quality Layer.
