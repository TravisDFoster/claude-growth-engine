# IC Trends — 2026-05-26

**Today's headline takeaway:** Two structural reads land together today: **Forrester Wave Intranet Platforms Q2 2026** finds that even at Leader vendors (Simpplr, Workvivo, LumApps, Unily), only 17 of 35 reference customers actually use the vendor's AI meaningfully — adoption gap, not capability gap. Pair that with **Gallagher's State of the Sector 2026**: 61% of orgs have no formal change-comms strategy and only 5% report optimized AI integration. The vendor AI race has out-run the buyer's ability to absorb it — Cerkl's "delivery + measurement on the channels you already have" wedge gets sharper, not duller, the longer that gap holds.

### 1. Forrester Wave: Intranet Platforms, Q2 2026 — vendor AI is largely shelfware
**Source:** Forrester   |   **URL:** https://www.forrester.com/blogs/the-forrester-wave-intranet-platforms-q2-2026-our-evaluation-of-13-key-vendors/   |   **Date:** 2026-04-09   |   **Category:** Analyst

### 2. Gallagher State of the Sector 2026 — 61% of orgs have no formal change-comms strategy
