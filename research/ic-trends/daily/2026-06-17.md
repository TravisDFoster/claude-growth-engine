# IC Trends — 2026-06-17

**Today's headline takeaway:** The day's signal converges on one fault line — how employees *actually* use AI, and whether comms teams surface that behavior through trust or try to govern it away. Two fresh HBR pieces (employees hide their AI use when psychological safety is low; the scarce skill is judgment, not speed) land against Staffbase's 2026 VOICES Awards reframing "trust and adoption" as employee experience's new differentiators — squarely in the governance-vs-measurement territory Cerkl is positioned to win.
