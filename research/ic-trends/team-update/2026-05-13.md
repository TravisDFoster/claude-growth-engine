## Headline of the week

**The IC category converged on a single narrative this week: "AI is producing more content, not better communication — the execution gap is the real problem."** Every major competitor — LumApps (new H1: "The leading AI Employee Hub"), Staffbase (VOICES Americas keynote), Poppulo (3 posts in 8 days), Workvivo (Seer launch), ContactMonkey (Cates' first quote: *"…what they really have is an execution problem"*), Firstup (AI as top-nav), plus today's sponsored Ragan webinar with LineZero — has now publicly adopted some flavor of this thesis. Cerkl **cannot differentiate by claiming the thesis** — the wave is already crowded. The next 2–3 weeks are the marketing window to claim **execution** as Cerkl's specific differentiator (delivery + read attribution + personalization across channels + free Foundations) before the consensus commoditizes. The other major opening: **0/10 competitors offer a free-forever tier**, and **none** are running product-led conquest SEO. Cerkl can own that lane unopposed.

## Action items (ranked)

### 1. Pick a sharp Cerkl tagline that claims *execution*, then test it everywhere
**What it is:** The "execution gap" framing is now consensus, but no vendor has staked a crisp one-liner around it. Closest attempts: Cates' *"execution problem"* quote, Workvivo's *"Your employees are talking. Are you actually listening?"*, Poppulo's *"How AI and Alignment Could Make or Break HR and Comms"* — all good directional signal, none owned by the speaker. See [W20 digest §"Pain point 1"](../competitor-marketing/weekly/2026-W20.md) and today's deep-dive findings.
**Why it matters for Cerkl marketing:** Cerkl's positioning ("the delivery + measurement layer across the channels you already have") *is literally the execution layer* — but the homepage and ads don't say that explicitly. The category has moved into Cerkl's lane and there's a 2–3 week window to own the language before someone else does.
**Action:** Tracy + Travis draft 3 candidate one-liners this week. Top candidate from the W20 digest: *"AI writes the message. Cerkl makes sure the right employee actually reads it."* A/B test as hero H1 + paid ad copy + a Travis LinkedIn essay within 14 days. Measure activation lift on Foundations sign-ups. Time-box: draft by 2026-05-16, A/B live by 2026-05-22.

Conquest SEO is escalating. **0/10 competitors offer a free-forever tier** ([W20 §"Pricing snapshot"](../competitor-marketing/weekly/2026-W20.md)).
**Why it matters for Cerkl marketing:** Cerkl can't out-spend on conquest. But Cerkl can publish *"Switch from [X] in an afternoon. Workshop ($3k/yr floor), AxiosHQ ($15k/yr floor), and ContactMonkey are the three competitors most exposed at the Foundations tier. Cheapest high-leverage move on the list.

### 3. Build the "Cross-Channel Read Gap" report — Cerkl-only-source data → gate → webinar funnel
**What it is:** Workvivo Seer's funnel is the cleanest one-week template in the category — stat-led ad → gated report (62%/49%/42%/39%) → 5/20 webinar → 5/21 NYC in-person event ([W20 §"Top 5 takeaways" #5](../competitor-marketing/weekly/2026-W20.md)). **Why it matters for Cerkl marketing:** Cerkl has structural data no single-channel competitor can produce: *what % of intended audience actually reads a given comm across email + intranet + Teams + Slack + mobile*. Competitors can frame the *problem* but can't quantify it. This is Cerkl's flagship gated-asset opportunity — and a venue to introduce the new tagline (Action #1) at scale.
**Action:** Decide on the data cut + headline stat by 2026-05-20. Draft report in 2 weeks; commission gated landing page + supporting webinar in 4. Owner: Marketing + data analyst. The report becomes the anchor for the next 90 days of paid creative and email nurture.
