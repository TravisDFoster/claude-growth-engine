Marketing Team — 7-Day Rollup

Date: 2026-06-17 (Wednesday) Action items: 7 Watchlist: 6

The IC category just crowded onto Cerkl's home turf — measurement. The "you can't prove your comms work" pain hook jumped from 3 vendors to 6 of 10 this week. That's validation — the buyer now expects measurement, exactly the wedge Cerkl has claimed — but it ends Cerkl's default ownership of the word: six competitors are planting flags on "measurement" with aggregate, borrowed stats while none delivers per-employee read attribution. The play: stop describing measurement and start out-specifying it — make the proof point a named employee opening a message, not a percentage. Two HBR pieces plus Gartner's anti-uniform-governance stance give Cerkl the empirical case that trust + measurement beats control.
