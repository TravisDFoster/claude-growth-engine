# Research at Cerkl

Research at Cerkl produces strategic intelligence that shapes marketing, product, and sales decisions. It's distinct from:

- **Analytics** — HubSpot/GA4 data; lives in `cerkl/hubspot/` and `marketing/skills/analytics-tracking/`.
- **Customer research** — interviews, JTBD, review mining; lives in `marketing/skills/customer-research/`.
- **Competitor profiling** — structured profile docs of named competitors; lives in `marketing/skills/competitor-profiling/` and feeds `shared/competitors.md`.

Research here is the **horizon-scan layer** — what's happening in the IC market that Cerkl should know about, regardless of whether anyone has asked.

## Streams

- **Internal-comms trends** (`ic-trends/`) — emerging patterns in the IC market, surfaced from publications, competitor blogs, analyst reports, and community discussion.

(More streams will land here as they're built — e.g., AI-in-IC tracking, partnership intel, buyer-narrative monitoring.)

## Voice and output norms

- **Audience is Travis.** First-person reading, tactical, no fluff.
- **Cite inline.** Every claim has a URL. No claim survives without a source.
- **Surface, don't summarize.** Travis reads originals when worth it. The recap exists to triage.
- **Signal over volume.** Most IC content is filler. The job is to elevate the 5–10 things that actually matter that day.
- **Cerkl angle when relevant.** If an item touches Cerkl's positioning, ICP, or competitive set, add a 1-line "Why it matters for Cerkl" — not on every item, only when it does.
