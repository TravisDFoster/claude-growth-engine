# Interactive Email Features

## Definition

In-email widgets that let recipients engage without leaving the message — emoji reactions, polls, surveys, eNPS responses, click-to-RSVP, embedded video. The IC platform category has converged on these as standard features.

## Why It Matters

Interactive features are how IC platforms differentiate against plain SMTP send tools and against the inbox itself. They also produce structured engagement data beyond opens and clicks, which feeds the measurement story IC teams use to defend budget.

## How It Works

[[contactmonkey]]'s 2026 benchmark ([[contactmonkey-internal-email-benchmark-2026]]) reports:

- **Emoji reactions:** 8% average click rate, 10% click-to-open rate
- **eNPS surveys:** 11% average click rate, 11% click-to-open rate

eNPS surveys outperform emoji reactions on click-through — suggesting employees engage more when there's an explicit ask than with passive reaction widgets.

## Tensions / Criticisms

- Click metrics on widgets can be vanity. A click-to-respond on an eNPS isn't the same as a meaningful signal of employee sentiment.
- Vendor data only; no independent validation.
