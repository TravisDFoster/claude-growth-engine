# Culture and Belonging

## Overview

Sense of inclusion, identity, and meaning at work — and how communications either supports or undermines it.

## Current Thesis

The negative-emotion stats in Gallup's 2025 data are a useful lens: daily loneliness at 22%, sadness 23%, anger 22%, stress 40% ([[state-of-the-global-workplace-2026]]). These are wellbeing-state measures, not engagement measures — and they suggest that even when engagement holds, the affective experience of work remains hard. Belonging-oriented IC interventions (recognition, peer connection, narrative coherence) target these measures more directly than they target the engagement number.
