## Summary
Gartner's Q1 2026 Quarterly Emerging Risk Report (n=337 senior risk and assurance executives) finds **information integrity risk** — driven by the spread of AI-enabled decision-making and uncertain AI transparency requirements — has risen to the **#1 emerging risk**. The survey also introduces **AI workforce preparedness** as a new critical risk and expands its spotlight on geopolitical uncertainty. The relevance to IC: when the enterprise's top risk officers say unverified, AI-generated content is the leading threat, the bar for *trustworthy, verifiable* internal messaging rises with it.

## Key Takeaways
- Information integrity (can we trust what's being communicated, and can we prove it?) is now the top-ranked emerging enterprise risk — an analyst-tier validation of the [[trust-and-credibility]] theme.
- The risk is explicitly tied to AI-generated/unverified content and unclear AI transparency rules — connects to [[ai-slop]] and [[shadow-ai-comms-problem]] on the IC side.
