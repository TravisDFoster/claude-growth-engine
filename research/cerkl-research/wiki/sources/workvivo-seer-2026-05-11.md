# IC Deep Dive — Seer by Workvivo

**Author:** Cerkl Internal Research (ic-trends deepdive)  |  **Published:** 2026-05-11 (T+5 days post-launch)  |  **Source type:** Internal competitive-intelligence deep-dive  |  **Original path:** `cerkl/research/ic-trends/deepdives/Competitors/workvivo-seer-2026-05-11.md`

## Summary

A Cerkl-internal deep dive on Workvivo's launch of **[[seer]]**, a standalone people-intelligence product launched 2026-05-06. Seer is built in-house and led by [[justin-black]] (founder of Microsoft/LinkedIn's [[glint]] People Science team). It competes in the employee-listening / people-intelligence category ([[glint]], [[culture-amp]], [[peakon]], [[qualtrics]]) — **not** in Cerkl's IC-delivery lane. The most consequential implication for Cerkl is *indirect*: Seer strengthens Workvivo's "full EX suite" framing without changing Cerkl's competitive positioning.

## Key Takeaways

- **Seer is a category-reframe play, not a feature launch.** Workvivo is naming a new sub-category ("[[people-intelligence]]") to differentiate from "employee listening." The wedge is in-product action — post updates, launch initiatives, create spaces — without leaving the listening tool.
- **[[justin-black]]'s hire is the credibility signal.** Black founded the People Science team at [[glint]] and led Glint inside Microsoft/LinkedIn. His public quotes anchor the launch narrative: *"The industry doesn't have a listening problem, it has an [[execution-gap|execution problem]]."*
- **Workvivo's full EX-suite framing strengthens.** With Seer, [[workvivo]] can pitch engagement feed + AI + people-intelligence in one vendor — a "one-vendor EX stack" pitch.
- **Reception so far is quiet.** As of T+5 days, no Reddit / LinkedIn-viral / Josh Bersin / Gartner / Forrester commentary. UC Today is the only trade-press editorial pickup. PR-driven rollout, not yet a market moment.
- **Not in Cerkl's lane.** Seer plays in people-intelligence; Cerkl plays in IC delivery + measurement. Adjacent categories; no Cerkl "vs. Seer" SEO page required.

## Notable Claims

**Launch details:**
- Product launched 2026-05-06.
- Available standalone or integrated into Workvivo. Source: https://www.workvivo.com/seer/ ; https://www.workvivo.com/newsroom/workvivo-launches-seer/
- Built in-house, not acquired.
- Leadership: Justin Black (Head of Seer, ex-Glint), Phil Murphy (Head of Growth, ex-Qualtrics), Jaime Gonzales (Principal People Scientist, ex-Microsoft/LinkedIn/Glint).

**Positioning quotes (Justin Black):**
- "The industry doesn't have a listening problem, it has an execution problem."
- "Measurement alone does not create progress… When feedback does not lead to visible outcomes, it breaks trust."
- "The next phase of this category is about impact and accountability, not just insight."

**Launch survey data (TrendCandy, 4,736 frontline + desk workers, surfaced in the press release):**
- 62% comfortable giving feedback
- 49% see meaningful change happen as a result
- 42% believe leaders are held accountable

**Product surfaces:**
- Customizable engagement surveys, pulse surveys, polls/quizzes spanning HQ and frontline
- AI theme detection on open-ended comments
- Segmentation by region, team, tenure
- Auto-generated executive reports
- AI-powered manager dashboards (personalized, mobile-first, guided next steps)
- In-product action: post updates, launch initiatives, create spaces directly from Seer
- Progress tracking on implemented changes

**Pricing:** Gated. Workvivo's procurement floor is ~$20k/year per existing internal references.
