# ContactMonkey's Internal Email Benchmark Report 2026

**Author:** Hetvi Mahida  |  **Publisher:** [[contactmonkey]]  |  **Published:** 2026-05-08  |  **Source type:** Vendor benchmark report (blog summary)  |  **URL:** https://www.contactmonkey.com/blog/contactmonkeys-internal-email-benchmark-report-2026-key-findings-for-internal-communications-teams

## Summary

ContactMonkey's annual internal-email benchmark report, based on 255,000 anonymized campaigns across 20+ industries. Top-line: **76% average open rate** and **9% average click rate**. Useful for benchmark citations in IC-measurement and email-channel blog posts, with the caveat that the data is single-vendor (ContactMonkey customers only) — authority is vendor-blog tier, not primary-research tier.

## Key Takeaways

- **76% average open rate is the headline benchmark** — roughly 3x typical external-marketing-email opens. The premium reflects audience captivity (employees obligated to open work email), not message quality.
- **Open rates decline with org size** — 89% (1–50 emp) → 73% (10,000+ emp). Larger workforces are harder to reach with broad sends.
- **Industry variance is wide**: top-quartile open rates (Legal/Real Estate 86%, Insurance 84%) vs bottom (Trucking 63%, Aviation 65%). Frontline-heavy industries cluster at the bottom — consistent with the broader frontline engagement gap ([[state-of-the-global-workplace-2026]], [[engagement-by-work-location]]).
- **Click-rate winners are unexpected**: Not-for-Profit and Insurance lead at 13%, ahead of "high-tech" sectors. Click-rate ranking does not mirror open-rate ranking — different underlying mechanism.
- **Interactive elements lift engagement** — eNPS surveys click at 11% click-to-open vs 10% for emoji reactions. Vendor-published; directionally consistent with the IC-platform trend toward in-email feedback widgets.

## Notable Claims

All statistics below cite https://www.contactmonkey.com/blog/contactmonkeys-internal-email-benchmark-report-2026-key-findings-for-internal-communications-teams.

**Overall benchmarks:** 76% average open rate across all industries; 9% average click rate across 255,000 campaigns.

**Open rates by industry:** Legal/Real Estate 86%, Insurance 84%, Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing 82%, Utilities 82%, Logistics/Supply Chain 67%, Airlines/Aviation 65%, Transportation/Trucking 63%.

**Click rates by industry:** Not-for-Profit 13%, Insurance 13%, Agriculture 11%, Construction 10%, Legal 10%, Leisure Travel/Tourism 10%, Airlines/Aviation 7%, Transportation/Trucking 7%, Media/Entertainment 7%.

**Open rates by org size:** 1–50 emp 89%, 51–200 emp 82%, 201–500 emp 80%, 501–1,000 emp 82%, 1,001–5,000 emp 78%, 5,001–10,000 emp 75%, 10,000+ emp 73%.

**Interactive features:** emoji reactions — 8% click rate, 10% click-to-open; eNPS surveys — 11% click rate, 11% click-to-open.
