# Seer (by Workvivo)

**Type:** Product (people-intelligence platform)  |  **Vendor:** [[workvivo]] (owned by [[zoom]])  |  **Launched:** 2026-05-06  |  **Head:** [[justin-black]]  |  **Website:** https://www.workvivo.com/seer/

## Overview

Seer is Workvivo's people-intelligence product, launched 2026-05-06. Available standalone or integrated into Workvivo. The wedge: **in-product action** — leaders can post updates, launch initiatives, and create spaces directly from Seer based on listening signals, without leaving the tool for a separate IC platform. Competes with [[glint]], [[culture-amp]], [[peakon]], [[qualtrics]] in the employee-listening / people-intelligence category.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Category-reframe via "people intelligence"** — Workvivo deliberately distinguishes from "employee listening."
- **In-product action as differentiator** — vs traditional listening tools that require export to a separate IC platform.
- **AI-powered theme detection** on open-ended comments.
- **AI-powered manager dashboards** — personalized, mobile-first, with guided next steps.
- **Auto-generated executive reports** for the people-strategy audience.
