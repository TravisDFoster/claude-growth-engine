# Daniel Shapero

**Type:** Executive (person)  |  **Role:** CEO, [[linkedin]] (2026–)

## Overview

Daniel Shapero became LinkedIn CEO after Ryan Roslansky moved to a Microsoft AI role in April 2026 ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/22/linkedins-ceo-is-moving-on-please-hold-your-tearful-video-tributes/)). His 2026-05-13 layoff memo (~875 cuts / ~5% of 17,500) is the first major change-comms moment of his tenure. Ragan's read in [[ragan-week-in-comms-linkedin-gm-gitlab-2026-05-15]] — credibly framed, formulaic. The memo names departing staff appreciatively and calls remaining staff to support them; uses the "reinvent how we work / agile teams" phrasing Ragan flagged as the 2026 layoff-template tell.

Important attribution note: the May 2026 LinkedIn layoff memo is **Shapero's, not Roslansky's**. Verify any Cerkl content citing it.
