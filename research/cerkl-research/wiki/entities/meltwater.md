# Meltwater

**Type:** Media intelligence / social listening / PR analytics platform  |  **Role:** Data partner on industry-association comms research; broader vendor in external comms / media monitoring  |  **Website:** https://www.meltwater.com

## Overview

Meltwater is a media intelligence and social-listening platform vendor whose primary product covers external comms (media monitoring, social listening, PR analytics). For IC research, Meltwater's relevance is as **data partner on the *Global Communication Report* series** co-published with [[iabc]] and [[usc-annenberg]] — the 2026 *A Quiet Shift* edition. Meltwater contributes media data to that research but is not itself an IC platform vendor and does not compete with Cerkl, ContactMonkey, PoliteMail, or the enterprise IC suite.
