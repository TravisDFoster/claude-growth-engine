# PR Daily

**Type:** Trade publication (Ragan Communications property)  |  **Coverage:** Internal communications, public relations, employee experience, AI in comms  |  **Website:** https://www.prdaily.com

## Overview

PR Daily is the trade-press publication for the internal-communications and PR field, owned by [[ragan-communications]]. Coverage spans practitioner interviews, IC strategy commentary, vendor news, conference coverage, and AI-in-comms reporting. For IC research: useful for practitioner-tier sourcing (interviews with named comms leaders, conference recaps) but not for benchmark citation — PR Daily is the editorial surface where many of Ragan's vendor-partner white papers get summarized, typically by the partner vendor's executive.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Practitioner-voice surfacing** — interviews with named IC leaders at large enterprises provide named-source quotes for blog citation.
- **Vendor-sponsored thought leadership** — many trend pieces are co-presented by IC platform vendors ([[workshop]], Staffbase, [[contactmonkey]], [[politemail]]). Useful but cite the sponsor.
- **Surfacing surface for Ragan / vendor co-branded white papers.** The PR Daily summary of the 2026 PoliteMail/Ragan benchmark ([[ragan-email-benchmarks-report-2026-05-12]]) is bylined by PoliteMail's CEO Michael DesRochers and labeled "presented by PoliteMail" — a clear pattern when reading benchmark coverage for citation purposes.
- **Ragan ecosystem hub** — links to Ragan training, certificates, and conferences.
