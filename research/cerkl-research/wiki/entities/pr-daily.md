# PR Daily

**Type:** Trade publication (Ragan Communications property)  |  **Coverage:** Internal communications, public relations, employee experience, AI in comms  |  **Website:** https://www.prdaily.com

## Overview

PR Daily is the trade-press publication for the internal-communications and PR field, operated by Ragan Communications. Coverage spans practitioner interviews, IC strategy commentary, vendor news, conference coverage, and AI-in-comms reporting. For IC research: useful for practitioner-tier sourcing (interviews with named comms leaders, conference recaps) but not for benchmark citation.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Practitioner-voice surfacing** — interviews with named IC leaders at large enterprises provide named-source quotes for blog citation.
- **Vendor-sponsored thought leadership** — many trend pieces are co-presented by IC platform vendors (Workshop, Staffbase, ContactMonkey). Useful but cite the sponsor.
- **Ragan ecosystem hub** — links to Ragan training, certificates, and conferences.
