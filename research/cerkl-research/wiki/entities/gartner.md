# Gartner

**Type:** Industry analyst firm  |  **Role:** CIO / CHRO / executive advisory; predictions, surveys, vendor evaluations (Magic Quadrant)  |  **Website:** https://www.gartner.com

## Overview

Gartner is the larger of the two major Western industry analyst firms (alongside [[forrester]]). For IC/EX research, Gartner's most-cited surfaces are (a) Predicts press releases tied to its HR practice, (b) the Gartner Global Labor Market Survey, and (c) the *Top Future of Work Trends for CHROs* annual series. In May 2026, Gartner published two HR-practice releases that anchor the 2026 [[ai-rollout-comms]] thesis — *People-Centric AI Strategy* (2026-05-13) and *AI Layoffs Don't Deliver Returns* (2026-05-05). Both releases use analyst-coined frames designed to travel: *enablement illusion*, *people-centric AI*, *people amplification*.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Predictions-as-frames** — Gartner-coined phrases like "[[enablement-illusion]]" and "[[people-amplification]]" are lifted verbatim across coverage; analyst-relations clearly engineers them for portability.
- **Global Labor Market Survey** — primary-research surface (12,004 employees/managers, 40 countries; 1Q26 wave) underwriting the HR-practice predictions.
- **CHRO repositioning of AI ownership** — May 2026 release explicitly names CHROs (with CIOs) as joint AI-strategy owners, a shift from the historical CIO-first frame.
- **Press releases as funnel content** — full reports are paywalled; the press release is the lead magnet. Methodology and detailed cuts live behind the subscription.
