# Matthew Prince

**Type:** Person (CEO) | **Role:** Co-founder & CEO, [[cloudflare]] | **Website:** cloudflare.com

## Overview
Matthew Prince is co-founder and CEO of [[cloudflare]]. In May 2026 he authored a Wall Street Journal op-ed defending layoffs made "despite record growth," articulating the bluntest CEO layoff rationale in the 2026 reference set: AI makes "measurers" obsolete while "builders and sellers" stay.

## Key Ideas / Contributions
- Coined (in practice) the [[measurers-vs-builders]] hierarchy — an explicit ranking of which employees AI makes expendable.
- Notable for *refusing* the standard reassurance script ("everyone is valued") — a deliberate culture-shift message per [[sean-devlin]]'s read.
- His framing is the sharpest live antagonist to IC's measurement-as-value argument ([[ic-measurement-and-roi]]).
