## Overview

Daren Jennings is CSO of intranet/EX vendor [[interact]]. He is the source of the two most quotable ideas in the [[ragan-engagement-to-experience-2026-06-11]] roundtable: the ROI-is-indirect admission about engagement, and the "comms leader as steward of institutional knowledge" thesis.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **ROI-is-indirect:** "engagement scores have been flat for 20 years and even declining"; "the R is farther away from the I than people want to see." Reverses the arrow — better work outcomes → higher engagement.
- **"Trusted action" intranet thesis** — future of the intranet is trusted action, not more content.
- **[[comms-as-knowledge-steward]]** — comms leaders become "the stewards of institutional knowledge… as the foundation to AI readiness."
