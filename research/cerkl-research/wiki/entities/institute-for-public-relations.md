## Overview

The Institute for Public Relations is an independent, nonprofit research institute focused on the science behind public-relations and communications practice. In the vault it is the research source behind [[ai-integrated-comms-function]] — its 2026 generative-AI research produced a **nine-item checklist for successful enterprise AI adoption** spanning culture, leadership, governance, and measurement. IPR is one of the few non-vendor, research-tier sources in the AI-in-IC space, which raises its citation authority above vendor surveys.
