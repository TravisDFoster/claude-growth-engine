# Forrester

**Type:** Industry analyst firm  |  **Role:** Buyer-side advisory; publishes the Forrester Wave evaluation reports across enterprise software categories  |  **Website:** https://www.forrester.com

## Overview

Forrester Research is one of the two major Western industry analyst firms (alongside Gartner). Its flagship vendor-evaluation product is the **Forrester Wave**, which scores vendors on current offering and strategy and visualizes the result as a quadrant placement (Leaders / Strong Performers / Contenders / Challengers). For internal communications, the most relevant Wave is the periodic *Intranet Platforms* evaluation, which sets the buyer reference for vendors like [[workvivo]], Staffbase, Microsoft Viva, Simpplr, Firstup, Unily.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Forrester Wave methodology** — vendor evaluations based on detailed scorecards plus reference-customer interviews.
- **Intranet Platforms Wave** — recurring evaluation; most recent edition Q2 2026 (prior was 2024).
- **AI-criteria mainstreaming** — the 2026 Intranet Wave introduces explicit AI-criteria categories (AI search, AI agents, AI admin, NL reporting), reflecting the category's pivot.
