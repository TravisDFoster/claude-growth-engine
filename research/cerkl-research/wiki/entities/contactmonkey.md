# ContactMonkey

**Type:** Internal-email platform vendor  |  **Role:** Outlook/Gmail-integrated internal-email tool with measurement and interactive widgets  |  **Website:** https://www.contactmonkey.com

## Overview

ContactMonkey is an internal-communications email platform that plugs into Outlook and Gmail to deliver employee comms with measurement, segmentation, and interactive widgets (emoji reactions, eNPS surveys, polls). The company publishes an annual internal-email benchmark report based on aggregate platform data — one of the more-cited benchmark sources in the IC industry, now sharing the 2026 benchmark stage with [[politemail]]'s parallel report (published 24 hours earlier via [[ragan-communications]]).

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Annual Internal Email Benchmark Report** — primary value to the IC research community is the aggregate benchmark data (open rates, click rates by industry, org size, interactive features).
- **Interactive email widgets** — pioneer of in-email engagement features like emoji reactions, eNPS surveys, polls.
- **Self-distributed benchmark (no Ragan co-brand)** — unlike PoliteMail's Ragan-co-branded distribution model, ContactMonkey publishes its annual benchmark directly. Same vendor-data substance; different distribution and editorial framing.
