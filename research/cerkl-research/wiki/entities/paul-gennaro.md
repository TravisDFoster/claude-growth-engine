# Paul Gennaro

## Overview

Paul Gennaro is CCO of [[new-york-life]] and an advisor to Ragan's Center for AI Strategy. He is the most senior in-house IC voice in the vault on *operationalizing* AI inside a corporate-comms function — author of a first-person account of 22 months integrating AI at a Fortune-100 insurer, mapped against [[institute-for-public-relations|IPR]]'s nine-item adoption checklist.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **AI-integrated comms as an operating model, not a toolset** ([[ai-integrated-comms-function]]) — "AI success is less about tools and more about how organizations operate."
- **Governance as a trust issue, not just a risk issue** — built an "Editorial Guidelines GPT" embedding standards into the workflow.
- **Outcome measurement over tool-usage metrics** (Lesson 8) — a practitioner-tier rebuttal to [[measurers-vs-builders]].
- **Human-in-the-loop discipline** — "AI accelerates production, but humans define the final product" ([[co-piloting]]).
