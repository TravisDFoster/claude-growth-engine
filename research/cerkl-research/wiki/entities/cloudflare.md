# Cloudflare

**Type:** Internet infrastructure / security vendor | **Role:** 2026 layoff-comms tone-axis escalation case | **Website:** cloudflare.com

## Overview
Cloudflare is a publicly-traded internet infrastructure and security company. In the IC research context it matters as a **2026 layoff-comms anchor case**: CEO [[matthew-prince]] used a WSJ op-ed to justify cuts (made "despite record growth") by declaring an entire job category — "measurers" — obsolete to AI.

## Key Ideas / Contributions
- Source of the [[measurers-vs-builders]] framing: "builders and sellers" are safe; "measurers" (much of marketing and finance) are what AI replaces.
- A tone-axis escalation in [[layoff-comms-choreography]] — abandons the "all employees equally valued" convention for an explicit value hierarchy.
- A direct public attack on the measurement discipline that underpins IC ROI ([[ic-measurement-and-roi]]).
