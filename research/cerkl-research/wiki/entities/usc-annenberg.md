# USC Annenberg

**Type:** Academic institution — Annenberg School for Communication and Journalism, University of Southern California  |  **Role:** Academic research partner for IC/comms industry studies  |  **Website:** https://annenberg.usc.edu

## Overview

USC Annenberg is the Annenberg School for Communication and Journalism at the University of Southern California — a major academic institution in communication research and the academic anchor of the *Global Communication Report* series co-published with [[iabc]] and [[meltwater]]. For IC research, USC Annenberg's contribution is **independent academic survey methodology** as a counterweight to vendor-funded benchmark research from [[politemail]], [[contactmonkey]], and the [[workshop]] series.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Academic anchor of *A Quiet Shift* (2026 Global Communication Report)** — surveys PR/comms pros on corporate voice and polarization. Free of vendor data interests; methodology is academic-survey, not platform-telemetry. March 2026 publication.
- **Research-tier authority for IC blog citation.** Sits above vendor and analyst tiers in citation discipline — primary academic research carries the highest sourcing weight in the [[ic-measurement-and-roi]] authority hierarchy.
