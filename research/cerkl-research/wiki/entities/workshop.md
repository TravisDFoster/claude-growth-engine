# Workshop

**Type:** Internal-communications platform vendor  |  **Focus:** Email-first internal-comms platform with multi-channel distribution and measurement  |  **Website:** https://www.useworkshop.com

## Overview

Workshop is an internal-communications platform vendor, focused on email-first multi-channel IC delivery with built-in measurement and segmentation. The company publishes an annual *Internal Comms Trends Report* surveyed across IC practitioners; the 2026 edition surfaced via PR Daily ([[six-internal-comms-trends-2026]]) is the primary source for many widely-cited 2026 IC stats — notably the 56%/4% manager-cascade gap.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Internal Comms Trends Report** — annual practitioner survey; one of the more frequently cited 2026 trend-stat sources.
- **Email-first IC platform positioning** — Workshop, [[contactmonkey]], Staffbase, and PoliteMail represent the email-centric tier of the IC platform category, distinct from intranet/feed-style platforms like [[workvivo]].
