# Ipsos Karian and Box (IKB)

**Type:** Employee-research / IC-measurement consultancy (Ipsos-owned) | **Role:** IC-specific trust & engagement research source

## Overview
Ipsos Karian and Box (IKB) is an employee-research and internal-communications measurement practice, now part of Ipsos. It runs large-scale employee-listening and IC-diagnostic work "across millions of employee responses," and is the data engine behind [[ghassan-karian]]'s trust argument. Parent **Ipsos** also publishes the **Global Trustworthiness Monitor**, cited as a non-"corporate-theatrics" complement to [[edelman]]'s Trust Barometer.

## Key Ideas / Contributions
- Finds a **recurring trust split**: employees trust peers and managers often; leaders and leadership comms "sometimes if at all."
- Diagnostic word-association data: employees describe leadership comms as "scripted," "insincere," "patronising," "cold," "evasive."
- Shows a **direct relationship between message candour and employee trust** — over-curated messaging makes trust plummet ([[ic-credibility-deficit]]).
- Where comms is experienced positively, leadership-trust confidence rises "into the eighties and nineties" — quantitative support for [[context-delivery]] and [[delivery-as-comms-strategy]].
- Sits in the [[ic-measurement-and-roi]] landscape as an **IC-specific trust-measurement** source (distinct from the single-channel email-benchmark vendors).
- **Employee-experience benchmark database** — described in [[karian-middle-manager-burnout-2026-06-04]] as "one of the largest in Europe." Sample basis for the 2026 finding that middle managers score significantly lower on manageable workload, decision influence, and wellbeing than other org levels; and that middle-manager work-life-balance scores deteriorated 2023–2025 while executive scores improved. Methodology details (panel design, refresh cadence, geography) not publicly disclosed.
- **2023 hybrid-work research** — IKB study Karian cites for the "manager-as-traffic-controller" coordination-load argument. Re-source if quoted directly.
- **Leadership diagnostics** — recurring IKB engagement product surfacing "responsible for everything but empowered for nothing" as a consistent middle-manager refrain.

## Headline products
- **IC Index** — annual UK study co-produced with [[iabc]]'s UK counterpart, the **Institute of Internal Communication (IoIC)**. 4th edition in 2026; **n=5,000 UK employees**. Public page: [ic-index.html](https://www.ioic.org.uk/knowledge-hub/thought-leadership-research/ic-index.html). Historical editions: 2023, 2024 ("Trust Issue"), [2025 PDF](https://ipsoskarianandbox.com/userfiles/ckeditor/reports/IC-Index-2025.pdf).
- **Employee-experience benchmark database** — the panel anchor for IKB's middle-manager + leadership claims; non-public.
- **Bespoke leadership diagnostics** — surface for individual-client engagements.
