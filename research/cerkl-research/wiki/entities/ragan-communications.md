# Ragan Communications

**Type:** IC/PR trade-publishing and training company  |  **Coverage:** IC, PR, employee experience, AI in comms; trade press + training + conferences + white papers  |  **Website:** https://www.ragan.com

## Overview

Ragan Communications is the trade-publishing and training company that owns [[pr-daily]] as its IC/PR property and operates the Ragan training, certification, and conference business. For IC research, Ragan plays two distinct roles: trade-press editorial (via PR Daily and ragan.com articles, which are useful for practitioner-tier sourcing) **and** a **co-branded white-paper distribution channel for vendor-authored benchmark research** (PoliteMail, ContactMonkey, others). The two roles share editorial framing but differ in source authority — the Ragan name on a white paper is editorial cover; the data is the partner vendor's.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Vendor-partner benchmark distribution.** Ragan hosts gated white papers branded as "Ragan reports" that are actually vendor-authored research. The "Ragan 2026 Internal Email Benchmarks Report" ([[ragan-email-benchmarks-report-2026-05-12]]) is [[politemail]]'s annual benchmark co-branded with Ragan distribution. Ragan has hosted [[contactmonkey]] benchmark white papers historically as well. Editorial-vs-sponsored ratio is not disclosed.
- **Trade-press editorial via PR Daily.** Practitioner interviews, conference recaps, and AI-in-comms coverage — useful for named-source quotes ([[austin-roth-eagle]] / [[cisco-comms-leader-ai-content]]) and trend reporting.
- **Training and certification stream.** Ragan training programs and the ECCC (European/Employee Comms) conference series are distinct revenue lines from the publishing business.
