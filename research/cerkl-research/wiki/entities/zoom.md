## Overview

Zoom is the enterprise-communications vendor best known for video conferencing. Its 2023 acquisition of [[workvivo]] expanded its surface into employee engagement / intranet platforms; with Workvivo's [[seer]] launch in 2026-05, Zoom now has a presence in employee-listening / people-intelligence as well. Bundling dynamics between Zoom Workplace and Workvivo are a recurring procurement-channel risk in IC-vendor competitive landscapes.
