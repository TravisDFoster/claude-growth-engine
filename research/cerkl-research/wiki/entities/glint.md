# Microsoft Viva Glint

**Type:** Employee-listening product (now part of Microsoft Viva)  |  **Parent:** Microsoft  |  **Origin:** LinkedIn (Microsoft acquired LinkedIn 2016)  |  **Website:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-viva/glint

## Overview

Microsoft Viva Glint is an employee-listening / engagement platform now bundled into the Microsoft Viva employee-experience suite. The product traces back to LinkedIn Glint (acquired by LinkedIn in 2018, then absorbed into Microsoft post-LinkedIn acquisition). Founded by people-science leadership including [[justin-black]] — who has since left Glint to head [[workvivo]]'s [[seer]] product.
