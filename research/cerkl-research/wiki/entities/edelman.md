# Edelman

**Type:** Global communications / PR firm | **Role:** Publisher of the annual Trust Barometer | **Website:** edelman.com

## Overview
Edelman is a global communications and PR firm best known in research circles for the **Edelman Trust Barometer**, an annual global trust survey. The 2026 edition supplies the headline IC-relevant trust data: employers are the most-trusted institution, even as trust turns insular.

## Key Ideas / Contributions
- Establishes "the employer" as the most-trusted institution (78% in 2026) — a strategic asset IC operates on top of.
- Names the CEO credibility gap (75% expect CEOs to bridge divides; 44% deliver).
- Frames trust as a **productivity variable** and the employer/IC as a [[trust-brokering|trust broker]].
- Functions as the global, brand-name anchor for the [[trust-and-credibility]] topic (contrast: [[ipsos-karian-and-box]] as the IC-specific research counterpart).
