# IABC

**Type:** Professional association — International Association of Business Communicators  |  **Role:** Practitioner association, certifications (CMP, SCMP), and co-publisher of independent IC/comms research  |  **Website:** https://www.iabc.com

## Overview

The International Association of Business Communicators is the global professional association for IC, PR, and business-communications practitioners. For IC research, IABC's most citable contribution is its **co-publication of independent academic research** with university partners (notably USC Annenberg) — the *Global Communication Report* series including the 2026 *A Quiet Shift* edition. Unlike vendor-funded benchmarks ([[politemail]] / [[contactmonkey]]), IABC research is academic-survey-based and free of vendor data interests.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Co-publisher of *A Quiet Shift* (2026 Global Communication Report)** with [[usc-annenberg]] and [[meltwater]] — surveys PR/comms pros on corporate voice and polarization (March 2026 release).
- **Practitioner certifications** (CMP, SCMP) — credentialing infrastructure that feeds practitioner-tier identity in the field.
- **Conference / publishing arm** — *Communication World* publication and annual World Conference; less-cited in current Cerkl research stream than [[pr-daily]] / [[ragan-communications]].
