# Workvivo

**Type:** Internal-communications / employee-experience platform vendor  |  **Parent:** [[zoom]] (acquired April 2023)  |  **Website:** https://www.workvivo.com

## Overview

Workvivo is an employee-communications and engagement platform — a modern social-feed-style intranet alternative — owned by Zoom since April 2023. It's been a Forrester Wave Leader in recent intranet-platform evaluations. In 2026-05, Workvivo launched [[seer]], a standalone people-intelligence product (engagement listening + manager-action surface), extending into the adjacent listening/insights category.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Modern social-feed intranet** — alternative to SharePoint-style intranets; emphasizes consumer-style UX and mobile-first frontline access.
- **EX-suite framing** — engagement feed + AI + (with Seer) people-intelligence in one vendor.
- **Zoom-bundled procurement** — sold alongside Zoom Workplace; bundling dynamics affect competitive positioning vs alternatives.
