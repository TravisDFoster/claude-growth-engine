# Interact

**Type:** Intranet / employee-experience platform vendor  |  **Focus:** Intranet + EX platform positioning as the "experience layer" across systems of record  |  **Website:** https://www.interactsoftware.com

## Overview

Interact is an intranet / employee-experience software vendor. In the IC research context it surfaces as a thought-leadership voice pushing the **engagement → experience** reframe and an "intranet as the experience layer where reliable information results in trusted action" thesis. Not on Cerkl's hardcoded competitor list (per [CLAUDE.md](../../CLAUDE.md)), but it is an intranet/EX platform — destination-framing, in the same family as the suites Cerkl positions the delivery-and-measurement layer against. Its claims are vendor-tier authority.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Engagement-vs-experience framing** ([[engagement-to-experience-pivot]]) — engagement = how employees feel; experience = the digital workplace that lets them do their best work.
- **"Trusted action" intranet thesis** — the future of the intranet is not more content but trusted action; the EX platform should be the experience layer across systems of record.
- **Comms-leader-as-knowledge-steward** ([[comms-as-knowledge-steward]]) — voiced by CSO Daren Jennings.
- **"Seven fundamental needs"** employee framework (referenced, not enumerated in the source).
