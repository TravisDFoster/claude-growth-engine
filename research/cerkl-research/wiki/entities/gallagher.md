# Gallagher

**Type:** Global insurance/risk/consulting firm with an IC consulting practice  |  **Role:** Publishes the annual *State of the Sector* IC industry survey (17th edition in 2026)  |  **Website:** https://www.ajg.com/employeeexperience/state-of-the-sector/

## Overview

Gallagher (Arthur J. Gallagher & Co.) is a global insurance and consulting firm whose Employee Experience consulting practice has published the annual *State of the Sector* report for 17 years — the longest-running practitioner survey of IC strategy, structure, channels, measurement, and (now) AI maturity. The 2026 edition surveyed 1,300+ IC practitioners across 40 countries. Distinct from email-specific benchmarks ([[politemail]] / [[contactmonkey]]): Gallagher's survey is *IC-wide* and *strategy-focused*, not platform-telemetry. It's the standard "industry context" companion to channel-specific benchmark reports.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Longest-running IC industry survey.** 17 editions; consistent methodology over time enables genuine longitudinal trend reading (cf. PoliteMail's vendor-data longitudinal series, which only covers PoliteMail customers).
- **Survey-based, not telemetry-based.** Strength: covers strategy, structure, and measurement framing — not just channel metrics. Weakness: self-report bias.
- **Authority tier:** practitioner-survey research, broader than vendor-data but narrower than primary academic. Sits in a useful middle tier for citation discipline.
