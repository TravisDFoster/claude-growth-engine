# LHH (Lee Hecht Harrison)

**Type:** Organization (global talent / career-transition firm; part of the Adecco Group)  |  **Role:** EVP-launch case ("a beautiful working world")  |  **Website:** https://www.lhh.com

## Overview

LHH (Lee Hecht Harrison) is a global talent-development, career-transition, and outplacement firm, part of the Adecco Group, operating in ~60 countries. In the vault it is the case organization for [[tony-kihl]]'s EVP launch — using the external brand promise of "a beautiful working world" as the internal employee-experience standard, with a *Beautiful Working World Hub* as the ongoing internal home.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- EVP run as a campaign / business transformation tied to the external value proposition.
- Listening-led design across 60 countries (surveys + localized roundtables).
- Self-reported outcomes: town-hall engagement 50–60% → 90–100%; engagement-survey results +20–30% in some areas.
