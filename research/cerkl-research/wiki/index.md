## Concepts
_Frameworks, theories, terms._

- [[ai-adoption-gap]] — Forrester 2026 finding: ~49% of intranet customers use vendor AI despite heavy investment
- [[ai-rollout-comms]] — Unifying 2026 thesis: bad rollout → shadow AI → no ROI → AI-attributed layoffs → choreography failure → deep listening emerges
- [[ai-slop]] — Roth-Eagle's framing: AI content fails when taste, judgment, and voice are absent
- [[ai-washing]] — Two senses: attributing cuts to AI when driver is elsewhere; or disclaimer-backfire (the GitLab case)
- [[cross-channel-benchmark]] — Structurally absent IC benchmark category; defensible content slot for vendors with multi-channel HRIS-synced telemetry
- [[deep-listening]] — Forrester's Feb 2026 category-naming: AI on collaboration exhaust → real-time sentiment; Cerkl's complement-not-compete positioning
- [[do-more-with-less]] — Persistent IC-team resource framing; 2026 shows practitioner pushback (44% have resources they need); now backed by Gartner's people-amplification rebuttal
- [[employee-engagement-three-bucket-model]] — Gallup's engaged / not engaged / actively disengaged
- [[employee-influencers]] — Trusted internal voices as IC amplifiers; 41% of teams actively investing
- [[employee-listening]] — Systematic collection and analysis of employee feedback
- [[enablement-illusion]] — Gartner-coined: leaders mistake adoption metrics for transformation; the "True ROI Index" rebuttal
- [[engagement-by-work-location]] — Engagement breakdown by remote / hybrid / on-site / non-remote-capable
- [[evergreen-content-strategy]] — Intranet-scope reframe: prioritize timeless reference content (policies, FAQs) over news
- [[execution-gap]] — Justin Black's frame: organizations collect feedback but fail to act, breaking trust
- [[interactive-email-features]] — In-email widgets (emoji reactions, eNPS, polls) and their measurement
- [[internal-email-benchmarks]] — Aggregate IC-email performance metrics; paired 2026 vendor anchors (PoliteMail/Ragan + ContactMonkey); methodology-caveated
- [[layoff-comms-choreography]] — Four-axis framework (tone / channel / timing / stakeholder-ack); the message *is* the choreography
- [[open-rate-inflation]] — Systematic overstating of IC email open rates from preview-pane, security-scanner, and stale-DL pixel hits
- [[people-amplification]] — Poitevin/Gartner-coined: AI ROI comes from amplifying people, not replacing them; analyst-grade counter to "cut to fund AI"
- [[people-intelligence]] — Workvivo's category-reframe term for next-generation employee listening
- [[shadow-ai-comms-problem]] — IC reframe of Gartner's 88% personal-AI-use finding; shadow AI is a comms problem with security consequences
- [[thriving-struggling-suffering]] — Gallup's wellbeing classification (distinct from engagement)
