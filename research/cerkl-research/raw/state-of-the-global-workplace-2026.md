## Methodology

- **Sample (2025):** 263,810 total respondents; 141,444 employed respondents
- **Historical scope:** 5,754,327 respondents across 2009–2025; 2,616,488 employed
- **Data source:** Gallup World Poll (surveys since 2005 across 160+ countries/areas)
- **Margin of Error (Global):** ±0.05 to ±0.08 percentage points at 95% confidence level
- **Regional data:** beginning 2025, based on three-year aggregated polling (prior reports used annual averages)
- **Target population:** civilian, noninstitutionalized adults aged 15+ employed by an employer
