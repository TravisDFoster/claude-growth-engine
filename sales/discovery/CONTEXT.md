# Discovery Context

## What we are building
Discovery call frameworks, qualification questions, and call prep guides for Cerkl Broadcast sales conversations.

## Qualification signals (paid tiers)

**Foundations+** (501–2,000 employees):
- Hitting Foundations send volume limits
- Need more than 3 communicator seats
- Need SSO or multiple email domains
- Need calendar invites or API access

**Omni AI** (2,001+ employees):
- Multiple channels in play or desired (email + Teams/Slack/intranet)
- Distributed or deskless workforce
- IT involvement required for data sync
- Need for AI personalization or omni-channel analytics
- 5+ communicator seats, 3+ instances

## Key discovery areas
1. **Current state** — how are they communicating with employees today? What tools?
2. **Pain** — what's broken or frustrating about the current approach?
3. **Scale** — employee count, locations, roles, deskless/frontline %?
4. **Channels** — email only, or also Teams/Slack/intranet?
5. **IT involvement** — SSO needed? HRIS sync? Who owns data?
6. **Timeline** — what's driving urgency right now?
7. **Competition** — are they evaluating anyone else?

## What good looks like
- Surfaces whether a prospect is a Foundations+, Omni AI, or self-serve fit within the first 15 minutes
- Questions that let the prospect articulate their own pain before the product is introduced
- Clear next step agreed at the end of every call
