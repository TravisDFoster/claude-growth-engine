# Outbound Context

## What we are building
Outbound prospecting sequences for Cerkl Broadcast targeting internal comms and HR leaders at mid-market and enterprise organizations (typically 500+ employees).

## Target personas
- Internal Communications Manager / Director
- Senior Director of Communications
- SVP of Internal Communications
- HR / People Experience leaders
- Office managers or chiefs of staff at SMBs (for Foundations-first outreach)

## Sequence approach
- Multi-touch cadences: email + LinkedIn
- Personalization triggers: company size, tech stack signals, job post signals, recent comms hire, relevant industry vertical
- Sequences should feel like a coherent conversation — not disconnected messages

## What good looks like
- Subject lines that earn the open without tricks
- Openers that reference a recognizable problem, not a product feature
- CTAs that are low-friction ("15 minutes" not "a demo")
- 3–5 touch sequences before pause — not 10+ spray-and-pray

## What to avoid
- Feature-first or product-first openers
- Generic "I wanted to reach out" openers
- Long cold emails (3+ paragraphs is too much)
- Pushy or assumptive language ("When can we meet?" as the opener)
- Sequences that pitch paid features to Foundations-fit prospects
