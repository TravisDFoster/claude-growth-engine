# Feature: Pulse Surveys & Employee Acknowledgments

> Part of Cerkl Broadcast. For product overview, see [broadcast.md](../broadcast.md).

**Available on:** All plans (Foundations, Foundations+, Omni AI)

---

## Pulse Surveys

Drag-and-drop survey blocks inserted directly into a blast. Let communicators collect quick employee feedback without a separate survey tool or external link.

Use cases: check-ins, sentiment captures, policy feedback, event RSVPs, quick polls.

---

## Employee Acknowledgments

Timestamped read receipts embedded into a blast. Employees confirm they've seen specific content — compliance communications, policy updates, safety notices, etc. — and the timestamp is recorded.

Acknowledgment tracking is built into Email Analytics: communicators can see who has acknowledged and who still needs to respond, and retarget non-acknowledgers with a follow-up blast.

---

## Why This Matters

Both features close the loop between sending a message and confirming it was received and acted on — without requiring a separate survey platform or manual follow-up process. This is particularly relevant for HR, compliance, and operational communications where confirmation of receipt matters.
