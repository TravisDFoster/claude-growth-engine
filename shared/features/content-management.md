# Feature: Content Management System (CMS / Content Hub)

> Part of Cerkl Broadcast. For product overview, see [broadcast.md](../broadcast.md).

**Available on:** Omni AI tier

---

## What It Does

A single system to plan, create, approve, and manage content for every channel. All content — regardless of which channel it ultimately publishes to — lives in one organized library.

---

## Key Capabilities

**Single Source of Truth**
One content library with automatic expiry and archival rules. Integrates RSS feeds from external sites so external content can flow into Broadcast automatically.

**Structured Content & Audience Targeting**
Customizable content fields: title, summary, links, attachments, categories, tags. Audience rules are applied once and reusable across channels — no re-targeting each time a piece of content is repurposed.

**Governance & Approvals**
Role-based permissions, staged approval workflows, audit trails, and policy locks on sensitive content. Designed for organizations that need editorial oversight before publication.

**Scheduling & Localization**
Content launches with end dates. Multi-language localization managed from a single record — auto-formatted for each channel destination. No duplicate records per language.

---

## Why This Matters for Omni-Channel

Without a CMS, publishing the same content across Teams, Slack, SharePoint, email, and mobile means manually re-entering and formatting it for each channel. The CMS is what makes the "create once, publish everywhere" model operationally sustainable at scale.
