# Feature: Internal Email Workflow — Blasts

> Part of Cerkl Broadcast. For product overview, see [broadcast.md](../broadcast.md).

**Available on:** All plans

---

## What It Does

The core email creation and delivery experience in Broadcast, built specifically for internal communicators. Not adapted from external marketing tools.

Sending speed: 25,000 emails per minute. No recipient limits. Layouts pretested on 60+ email clients — no broken rendering in Outlook or Gmail, no HTML hacking required.

---

## Building

- Drag-and-drop email builder with responsive, email-safe building blocks
- Pre-built template library and custom template support
- HTML upload for fully custom designs
- Personalization: add any employee attribute to the email body or subject line; fallback behavior for missing data fields
- Translation: 133 languages supported ("Duplicate & Translate" workflow powered by Google Cloud Translation API; Beta feature; ineligible: already-translated, scheduled, or HTML-upload blasts)

---

## Sending & Targeting

- Audience targeting using dynamic segments from Audience Manager
- Scheduled sends
- Attachment support
- **Retargeting** — follow-up sends to non-engagers from a prior blast
  - Trigger options: not opened / not clicked / neither opened nor clicked
  - Schedule window: up to 180 days after the original send
  - System dynamically removes employees who engage before the retarget date
  - Separate analytics tracked for the retargeted blast
