# Feature: Omni-Channel Publishing

> Part of Cerkl Broadcast. For product overview, see [broadcast.md](../broadcast.md).
> For the content management system used to publish across channels, see [content-management.md](content-management.md).

**Available on:** Omni AI tier

---

## What It Does

Create once, distribute across all channels employees already use — without duplicate posting, channel-by-channel re-entry, or content reformatting. Each channel receives the appropriately formatted version of the message.

---

## Channels

### SharePoint
Delivers SharePoint content into Teams, Slack, mobile app, and personalized newsletters automatically. Cross-channel engagement is tracked back to the SharePoint source. Implemented as a Microsoft-approved SharePoint web part.

Solves a common problem: SharePoint content often goes unread because employees don't navigate to it. Broadcast pushes it to where employees already are.

### Microsoft Teams
Delivers hyper-personalized updates, alerts, and announcements directly inside Teams — without flooding channels with generic blasts.

Employees install the Broadcast app from the Microsoft Teams app store and sign in with their work email. Admins can pin the app across Teams instances through admin settings. Available on both desktop and mobile Teams.

Hybrid personalization: rules-based targeting for critical communications + ML personalization based on engagement patterns.

### Slack
Publish to Slack from Broadcast's centralized CMS in one click. Messages are delivered as targeted to the right audiences and personalized per employee. Employees receive content as part of their normal Slack workflow without additional logins.

### Mobile App
Built for deskless employees and frontline workers — including those without corporate email addresses.

- Personalized push notifications targeted by role, location, or any employee attribute
- Tracks opens, clicks, and engagement by audience
- Syncs with all other Broadcast channels for a consistent personalized experience

### Microsites
Personalized employee news hubs that aggregate content from email, Teams, Slack, and SharePoint into a single, searchable, always-available site tailored by role, department, or location. Accessible from any device at any time.

Solves information fragmentation — employees have one place to find any update instead of searching across channels.

## Positioning

Broadcast's positioning is "a communications layer — not a replacement for existing infrastructure." Lower implementation complexity than intranet-based competitors. It sits on top of the channels companies already have.
