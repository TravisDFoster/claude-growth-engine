# Feature: Analytics & Insights

> Part of Cerkl Broadcast. For product overview, see [broadcast.md](../broadcast.md).

---

## Email Analytics — All Plans

Available on Foundations, Foundations+, and Omni AI.

- **Engagement by Audience** — reach, activity, and behavior tracking per segment
- **Campaigns** — connect related emails to measure the impact of a larger initiative
- **Performance Over Time** — trends across reporting periods and custom date ranges
- **Acknowledgment Tracking** — see who has acknowledged and who still needs to respond
- **Segment-based reporting** — filter results to specific audience groups
- **Campaign rollups** — aggregate metrics across multiple blasts
- **Shareable dashboards** — role-based access; administrators have full visibility, team members can selectively share access

Broadcast describes this as "a single source of truth for internal email" — real engagement, not just opens.

**Redesigned analytics experience released May 12, 2026.**

---

## Omni-Channel Analytics — Omni AI Only

Extends analytics across all channels with unified, cross-channel dashboards.

- **Unified Channel Performance** — compare email, intranet, Teams, Slack, and mobile side-by-side: delivery, reach, engagement
- **Audience & Personalization Insights** — drill into performance by role, location, department, language, or custom attributes
- **Content Intelligence** — actionable recommendations on topics, formats, and headlines that drive outcomes (not just raw charts)
- **Outcome & Compliance Tracking** — monitor acknowledgments; track who has seen and acted on critical updates; identify underused channels and content gaps
