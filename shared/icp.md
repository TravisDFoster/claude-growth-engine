# ICP Quick Reference


## Two Distinct ICPs

### Foundations / Subscriber Growth ICP
Any team that owns internal employee communication and needs something better than Outlook or Gmail. Defined by need state, not headcount — includes SMBs and organizations of all sizes.

**Buyer:** Whoever owns employee communication at that org. Ranges from a formal internal comms director to an office manager, chief of staff, operations lead, or founder at smaller companies.

### Paid ICP (Foundations+, Omni AI)
Mid-market to enterprise organizations with messy internal comms environments — distributed, frontline, deskless, or global workforces; multiple channels to coordinate; need for segmentation, analytics, and IT-friendly data sync.

**Buyer:** Internal Communications Managers/Directors, Senior Directors of Communications, SVPs of Internal Communications, HR/People Experience leaders. IT is often a co-buyer.

**Paid customers range from 2,000–12,200+ employees.**

---

## Upgrade Path by Company Size

| Company Size | Expected Behavior |
|---|---|
| 0–500 employees | Stay on Foundations. Low send volume, simple needs. Subscriber growth is the goal. |
| 501–2,000 employees | Likely to upgrade to Foundations+ (unlimited send volume, unlimited seats, SSO). |
| 2,001–5,000 (complex comms stack) | Likely to upgrade to Omni AI. Trigger: multiple channels, diverse audiences, deskless workers. |
| 5,000+ employees | Likely to upgrade to Omni AI regardless of stack complexity. |

---

## Buyer Titles

**Paid buyers:** Internal Communications Managers/Directors, Senior Directors of Communications, SVPs of Internal Communications, HR/People Experience leaders. IT is often a co-buyer.

**Foundations buyers (including SMB):** Whoever owns employee communication — formal internal comms or HR leader at larger companies; office manager, chief of staff, operations lead, or founder/CEO at smaller ones.

**End users:** Employees — office staff, frontline workers, field teams, employees without corporate email.

---

## Primary Use Cases

Primarily **internal communications**, with strong adjacent use cases in:
- HR communications
- Employee experience
- Change/compliance communications
- Operational communications

Designed for internal communicators, not marketing teams.

---

## Key Qualification Signals (Paid)

- 500+ employees (Foundations+), 2,000+ (Omni AI)
- Multiple channels in play or desired (email + Teams/Slack/intranet)
- IT involvement or requirement for SSO/data sync
- Dissatisfaction with Outlook/Gmail, a homegrown tool, or a competitor
- Distributed workforce, frontline workers, or deskless employees
- Need for dynamic audience segmentation without IT dependency
