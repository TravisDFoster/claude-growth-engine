## How Broadcast Differentiates

Cerkl's positioning is not "we are the company intranet." It is: "We are the personalized delivery and measurement layer across the channels you already have."

- Less emphasis on replacing existing digital workplace tools
- More emphasis on audience sync, personalization, native inbox delivery, cross-channel measurement, low IT friction, and faster launch
- Free Foundations plan is a meaningful commercial differentiator vs. heavier enterprise suites

> **2026-06-17 watch — the measurement axis is converging on Cerkl's core ground.** The "you can't prove your comms work" pain hook jumped from 3 to **6 of 10 vendors** in one week (Firstup *"$1T turnover"*, AxiosHQ *"92/90/82"*, ContactMonkey *"demonstrate clear impact"*, Simpplr *"282% ROI"*, Poppulo *"analytics… what's working"*, Workshop *"less guesswork"*). This validates Cerkl's thesis but ends Cerkl's *default* ownership of the measurement word. **Counter-move: out-specify them.** Competitors claim measurement with aggregate ROI/percentage stats; none delivers *employee-level read attribution* ("delivered, opened, read, by name"), and their own G2 reviews complain about the analytics/customization ceilings that undercut the claim. Cerkl's wedge sharpens from "we measure" to "we measure *per employee* — ask the others for that and watch the demo end." Source: [`competitor-marketing/weekly/2026-W25.md`](../research/competitor-marketing/weekly/2026-W25.md). **2026-06-24 update — the axis migrated from hero claim to the PROOF/substantiation layer.** Homepage heroes rotated to "one platform + AI" (Staffbase's new H1, 7/10 consolidation framing), while measurement moved *down-funnel* into ad creative (now 7/10, still climbing — LumApps added *"deep analytics prove what's working"*) and, critically, into **gated ROI-proof assets**: Firstup published a **Forrester TEI study claiming 398% ROI / $8.9M savings**, ContactMonkey re-pushed an **Internal Email Benchmark Report 2026**, Simpplr ran an adoption-metric case-study roundup. ## What Customers Switch From

Cerkl more often replaces patched-together internal comms stacks than named enterprise suites:
- Outlook/Gmail newsletter workflows
- PDFs and spreadsheets / distribution lists
- Homegrown tools
- Disconnected email-plus-intranet processes

Recurring switch reasons: better design, better scheduling, dynamic targeting, personalization, cross-channel reach, measurable analytics. The dominant pattern is "escape the manual stack," not displacing an enterprise competitor.

## Head-to-Head Positioning

### vs. Staffbase
Staffbase is a fuller intranet platform — if a buyer wants to replace their entire digital workplace, Staffbase competes there. Cerkl's advantage: faster to launch, less IT lift, no channel replacement required, self-serve free entry point. Cerkl loses when the buyer's primary need is a home-base intranet, not delivery and measurement.

**Cerkl reframe (use this in deals where Staffbase is the alternative):** *"We're the delivery + measurement layer. The intranet is the destination, not the comms strategy."* Lean into speed-to-launch and IT-light implementation against Staffbase's ~$30k floor and multi-quarter rollouts. The 2026 Staffbase narrative is upstream — Navigator (permission-aware AI search), AI quality layer, intranet-as-content-source — which makes their pitch governance-heavy and longer to procurement; Cerkl's pitch lands inside the IC team's existing channels with measurable delivery from day one. (Reframe sourced from the 2026-05-10 deep-dive: [`Competitors/staffbase-2026-05-10.md`](../research/competitor-marketing/Competitors/staffbase-2026-05-10.md).)

### vs. Simpplr
Simpplr crossed from "intranet competitor" into direct orchestration in **February 2026** with **Comms AI** — an AI workspace for IC covering campaign planning, multichannel delivery, and approvals. Stack that on top of Gartner MQ Leader (3rd year), Forrester Wave Q2 2026 Leader, and 4.6/5 G2 average, and Simpplr is the most material 2026 competitive shift in the space. **Cerkl reframe:** *"Comms AI is a comms workspace built on top of Simpplr's intranet.
